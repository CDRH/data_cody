      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>

      <p>Yours from Omaha <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="received​ ">Recd</a>
    ​— Now George we must not let any one get <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="ahead​ ">a head</a>

      <p>I heard from <span class="persName">Hymer</span> he sent reports. And I tell you That Man Hymer Knows his business. And George— without him we would have been holding the bag— And I hope all concerned will appreciate what he has done.</p>
