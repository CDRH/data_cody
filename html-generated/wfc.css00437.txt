      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">

      <p>Yours from Omaha <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="received​ &#xA;    ">Recd</a>— Now George we must not let any one get <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="ahead​ &#xA;    ">a head</a> of us on the land we propose to irrigate on either side of the river.<a href="#n02" class="internal_link">2</a> I fear you <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="do not​ &#xA;    ">donot</a> appreciate that <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="enterprise​ &#xA;    ">enterprize</a> as much as I do. And if the Paxton party <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ &#xA;    ">dont</a> get up there soon And decide whether they are going into it or not we will <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="lose​ &#xA;    ">loose</a> at lot of our land.<a href="#n03" class="internal_link">3</a> We must get to work and do something and that right away.</p>

to the wheel and hoof things up— And let me know how the thing stands.</p>

      <p>I heard from <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/hymer.w">Hymer</a>
         </span> he sent reports. And I tell you That Man Hymer Knows his business. And George— without him we would have been holding the bag— And I hope all concerned will appreciate what he has done.</p>

      <p>He will need some money at once. We must keep our credit good and at once— Nothing will hurt us more than to <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="lose​ &#xA;    ">loose</a> our credit. Will you let me hear from you at once. </p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours— </span>

         <br/>Cody
