      <p>In reply to your letter about Millis &amp; Co<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a> and others from Red Lodge<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a>. I sent <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span> on the 16<u>th</u> Sep- $5.000 asking him to pay all Red Lodge debts<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a>- since then I have telegraphed him three times and asked him to answer at my expense if he had paid them— Not a word can I get out of him. If our Co gets into trouble it will be Algers neglect— for he has never called on me for money and failed to get it—</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Col</span>
