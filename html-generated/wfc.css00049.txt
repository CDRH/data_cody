      <span class="salute">My. Dear. Sam.</span>


      <p>Yours recd. I met <span class="persName">Ingraham</span> yesterday. Do you know I begin to <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="believe​ ">beleive</a>
    ​ like your self that he is a bad egg. or that he is a forked tongue. he told me. or at least wrote me he had placed your MSS. in B.&amp;. A. hands. Yesterday he Says B.&amp;.A. don't want them. But that he has them ready. whether he has or not I cant

I have paid out so much money of late in buying cattle that I am pushed for ready funds at present. I do hope you will come out right side up yet. My <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="companies​ ">companys</a>
    ​ is all made up for this season.</p>

      <p>I will not have the time to come and see you this fall. until I play there in Dec. Give my love to Seal &amp; family.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Your Friend.</span>
