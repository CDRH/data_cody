      <span class="salute">Dear. Sister &amp; Brother—</span>


      <p>Thought I would write you a few lines while waiting for breakfast. I have had a hard summers work and have not made much money so my expenses has been big and it being a new show had to advertise it largely but next season. I expect to clear a fortune with it. I am working my way home. or at least west. will close in

Omaha in October-</p>

      <p>Well I have got out my petition for a divorce with that woman as long as I am going to. she has tried to ruin me financialy this summer Oh I could tell You lots of funny things how she has tried to bust up the horse ranch and buy more property &amp; get the deeds in her name &amp;c— Cousin Sophia since her daughter was out there has changed her tune.</p>

      <p>Well I hope this will find you all well—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Love</span>

         <br/>Brother<br/>
Will
</span>


      <p>Our Cleveland suit comes up in January before the United States Court. Met Loucindas Conyers lawyer here

he feels confident of our winning.</p>

      <span class="ab">Will</span>
