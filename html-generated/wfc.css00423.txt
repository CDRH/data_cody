      <span class="salute">Dear Tait—</span>



      <p>Fine weather. A dandy. Show— on <u>Preparedness</u> which catches the people— We are on a fine Base Ball Park— Use their grand stand which seats 15000 people we have had her nearly full <del>to d</del> twice to day sunday will fill it—</p>

      <p>Ringlings hammering us. But this word <u>Preparedness</u> draws like a plaster—</p>

      <p>You let me hear from you savey?</p>

      <p>I am feeling splendid</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Governor
