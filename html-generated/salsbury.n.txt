   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink"
         href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/salsbury.n">Salsbury, Nathan, 1846-1902</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">Nathan Salsbury (1846-1902) was a veteran of the Civil War who later became an actor and a successful theatrical producer and manager. He joined Cody in 1884 as co-owner of Buffalo Bill's Wild West. His show business skills contributed greatly to the venture's success until his death in 1902.</div>
