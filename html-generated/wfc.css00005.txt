      <span class="dateline">Glasgow
Aug 5th/04</span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear Bill,</span>


      <p>I will let you know about bringing Indians soon as I can hear from my agents in Paris—in two or three weeks. We are after a great Building there.</p>

      <p>My business is simply great. We will get pretty near to $70.000 here this week.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Your friend</span>
         <br/>Bill Cody</span>
