      <p>
         <div class="center">LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT OF <span class="persName">HORACE C. ALGER</span>.</div>
      </p>

      <p>IN THE NAME OF GOD, AMEN;</p>

      <p>I, HORACE C. ALGER, being of sound mind and memory but knowing the uncertaintynof human life, do now make and publish this my last will and testament, that is to say;</p>

      <p>All the property I may be possessed of at time of death I will and bequeath to my three sisters after all my debts have been first paid, infull out of said property or estate.</p>

      <p>HORACE C. ALGER. (seal)</p>

      <p>Signed, Sealed, Published and Declared by the said Horace C. Alger the testator as and for his last will and testament; and we at <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="​ ">j</a>
    ​ his request and in his presence and in the presence of each other have hereunto subscribed our names as witnesses thereto, thus 23 rd day of January, A.D. 1894.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">WITNESSES.</span>

         <br/>P. L. GIBBS.
<br/>William J.Thom.
<br/>George Woodward.</span>
