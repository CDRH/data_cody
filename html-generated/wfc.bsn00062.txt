      <div class="note">Re. Cody Stmt.</div>


      <span class="dateline">July 14, 1913.</span>


      <span class="persName">Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

696 Ellicott Square,

Buffalo, New York,


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:-</span>


      <p>I enclose herewith stament of business transacted by the Lincoln Land Company, with respect to the half interest in property owned joint with it, at Cody, which you represent as trustee, for the six months ending June 30, 1913, the same showing a balance due the Lincoln Land Company of $1180.86.</p>

      <p>If it is possible, we would like to receive a substantial payment on this balance, as we are greatly in need of funds to meet some large disbursements, which mature August 1st, next.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours,</span>


         <br/>W. W. Turner<br/>

Secretary.

Enc. StMt.<br/>

CLB<br/>


         <div class="note">Sent them $600.00 on July 23. 1913<br/>BR.</div>
