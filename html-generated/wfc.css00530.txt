   <div class="note">Note 1: "Buffalo parties" refers to the investors from Buffalo, N.Y., primarily <span class="persName">Bleistein</span>, <span class="persName">Gerrans</span>, and <span class="persName">Rumsey</span>.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "Bridge" refers to Cody's efforts to finance building a bridge over the Shoshone River near Cody.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: "Flats" refers to acreage in the South Fork valley south of Cody, Wyoming, that would later become known as "Irma Flats," named after William F. Cody's youngest daughter. </div>
