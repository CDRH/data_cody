      <span class="dateline">Memphis. Oct 4th 97<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>
      </span>

      <p>The Directors<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> want to know why construction for Aug was so much and no construction done— You should get expenses down out there. it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ ">wont</a>
    ​ do. you must have a number of people on salary doing nothing. And the rest soldiering on the company. The directors ask me why the expenses are so high. I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ answer them.</p>

      <p>Will do all I can to get Jones<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> out there with men who will buy—</p>

      <p>Hope the placer<a href="../doc/n04" class="internal_link">4</a> will be a success— I am worn out took 24 hours to get here Two weeks now of long runs. I will be glad to get out there—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours—</span> 

         <br/>Cody</span>

   </div>



   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Memphis, Tennessee, on October 4, 1897. </div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "Directors" are the investors of Shoshone Irrigation Company, including <span class="persName">Bleistein</span>, <span class="persName">Gerrans</span>, and <span class="persName">Rumsey</span>.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: C. B. Jones was a land agent from Galesburg, Illinois, who brought settlers to the Big Horn Basin.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 4: Beck filed placer mining claims for Cody and other directors of Shoshone Irrigation Company.</div>
