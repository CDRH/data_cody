      <span class="dateline">Memphis. Oct 4th 97<a href="#n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>
      </span>

      <p>The Directors<a href="#n02" class="internal_link">2</a> want to know why construction for Aug was so much and no construction done— You should get expenses down out there. it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> do. you must have a number of people on salary doing nothing. And the rest soldiering on the company. The directors ask me why the expenses are so high. I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> answer them.</p>

      <p>Will do all I can to get Jones<a href="#n03" class="internal_link">3</a> out there with men who will buy—</p>

      <p>Hope the placer<a href="#n04" class="internal_link">4</a> will be a success— I am worn out took 24 hours to get here Two weeks now of long runs. I will be glad to get out there—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours—</span> 

         <br/>Cody</span>
