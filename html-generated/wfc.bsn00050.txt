      <div class="note">Re sending statement, <br/>

CODY.  </div>


      <span class="dateline">January 29, 1910.</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>,

680 Ellicott Square,

Buffalo, New York.


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:</span>


      <p>Herewith the usual semi-annual statement, showing business transacted with respect to the property in Cody, held jointly by the Lincoln Land Co. and yourself, as trustee, representing sundry interests, showing a balance of $612.06 due the Lincoln Land Co.</p>

      <p>I hope the county division matter will soon be settled, so that Cody will be in shape to go ahead. We think there is no doubt, if the decision is favorable to Cody, that it will create quite a movement in real estate.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly,</span>


         <br/>ABMinor<br/>

Secy.

<div class="note">MAR

Enc. statement.</div>
      </span>

      <div class="note">This a copy of letter sent me by Lincoln Land Co.<br/>
         <del>Bronson Rumsey</del>
