      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>

      <p>Yours of the 11<u>th</u> Yes we are all discouraged about the Land department<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> but its a department that must be kept up; its our only hope— If Elliot<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a>

buy it. have you got any photographs of the country taken this time of year— I have sent for some folders and I want some photographs as many as I can get. I am going to rig up a stand at my front entrance<a href="../doc/n05" class="internal_link">5</a>— &amp; see if I can do some <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="advertising​ ">advertiseing</a>
    ​. Send photographs soon as possible.</p>

      <p>I have just had the worst three weeks of rain I ever had in the show business. And the time we have getting on and off the lots our big <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="wagons​ ">waggons</a>
    ​ mire to the beds tonight every stringer jack &amp; seat had to be carried on the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="men's​ ">mens</a>
    ​ shoulders to the road. We have an 88 mile run out of here if we get to <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="tomorrow's​ ">tomorrows</a>
    ​ town by noon we will be in luck. talk about men working on ditch. These men of ours simply work day &amp; night.  We get wet every day in fact <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="haven't​ ">havent</a>
    ​ been dry but twice for three weeks—</p>

      <span class="closer">Yours— 

Cody</span>
