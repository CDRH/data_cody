Feby 12 —1914</span>

      <span class="salute">Mr Tate:</span>


      <p>I received the copies of the Post you kindly sent me for which I thank you very much. I find there will not be enough to go around and will ask you to send me about six or eight more copies if the issue is not <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="exhausted​ ">exausted</a>
    ​.</p>

      <p>There is enclosed $1.00. pay for the papers and then you and Hank go out and take something for the change.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very Truly Yours</span>

         <br/>John R. Brennan
