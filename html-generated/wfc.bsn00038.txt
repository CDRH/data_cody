      <div class="note">Enc. Statement. </div>


      <span class="dateline">July 16th, 1906.</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

78 Exchange Street,

Buffalo, New York.


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:-</span>


      <p>Herewith statement of account of the Cody property held jointly by this Company and yourself as Trustee representing certain other interests, showing net balance for the one-half represented by you $5424.79, for which amount you will find herewith New York draft payable to your order. </p>

      <p>Please receipt and return the accompanying voucher, and oblige,</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>

         <br/>ABMinor<br/>
Sec'y.


<div class="note">Enclo. Stmt., Dft. &amp; Voucher.</div>
