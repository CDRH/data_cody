      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined With Pawnee Bill's Great Far East</div>

      <span class="dateline">Carrollton Mo
Sep. 25<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Clarence</span>


      <p>Col Dyer was with Me when your good letter came. And he appreciated when you said we would cut out the Wyoming hunt if our mines. demanded our attention He has a monied friend in Boston he has invited to go out with us as soon as he hears from him he will get you two together</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Hastly Yours</span>

         <br/>Col.
</span>

      <p>We cant get Deer Bear. &amp; quails <del>ne</del> at Campo Bonito<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a>
      </p>
      <div class="note">Col Dyer-uses a No 12 Winchester Pump shot gun<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>
      </div>
