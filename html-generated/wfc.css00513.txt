      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>—</span>

      <p>Here is some good news.  R. Law<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a> is the supt of Burlington R.R. in Iowa. Mr Law is the supt who has opened up the coal fields and built roads to them for the Burlington in Missouri &amp; Iowa<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a>— And is <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="really​ &#xA;    ">realy</a> the coal expert for the Burlington and the practiced rail road builder. Mr Law is coming out with me to look the coal fields<a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">5</a> over. And the most practical route to the Park<a name="n06.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n06.note" class="ref">6</a> from what I can learn they will soon push through our country. If they do you know what that means to our country. We leave Omaha Nov 6<u>th</u>
      </p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Write me often</span> 

         <br/>Col</span>
