      <h3>Buffalo Bill's Injuns.</h3>

      <p>About twenty Indians from Pine Ridge agency in charge of Major Burke, Buffalo Bill's lieutenant, arrived here this morning. They were joined by an equal number of ex-hostiles from Fort Sheridan, and the aggregation later left for New York en route for Europe to join the Wild West show.</p>
