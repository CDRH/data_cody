      <p>I might get some good men to go out and look our proposition over. But I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ &#xA;    ">dont</a> want to send them if your crops of this year <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> stand inspection.       <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
