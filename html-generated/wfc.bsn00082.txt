      <span class="dateline">Feb. 28, 1917.</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. H. M. Gerrans</span>, 

Hotel Iroquois,

Buffalo, N.Y.


<span class="salute">Dear Mr. Gerrans:</span>


      <p>We enclose <span class="persName">Mr. Bleistein's</span> statement, $4077.43, which included interest to February 27, 1917.  Mr. Bleistein will find this statement correct.</p>

      <p>We have had a very cold bad winter, However, it looks now as though the weather conditions will permit business to open up. It has been very quiet during the extremely cold weather.</p>


      <p>With kind regards I remain,</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>

CODY TRADING CO

<br/>Jake<br/>

Mgr.
