      <span class="dateline">April 19 1908<a name="n01.ref"> </a>

      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>—</span>


      <p>Did you do anything about a petition to the President<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a> about the high line canal<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a>? </p>

      <p>I will play Washington<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a> May 18th &amp; 19th.  And if I can do anything by seeing the President let me know. I would be pleased to have a letter from you.</p>

      <p>The old Wild West will open the 21st.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>
