   <div class="letter">


      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>
      <span class="dateline">

Pittsburg

May 29<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">My. Dear Elwell<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a>
      </span>



      <p>Glad to get your nice letter—From camp—Its strange to me—that people cant learn to <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="always​ &#xA;    ">allways</a> set there camps in <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="secluded​ &#xA;    ">se cluded</a> places out of the wind. This should be done— Speak to Mr Kelsey. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="also​ &#xA;    ">allso</a> try and locate the camp where there is grass—I am surprised that active work was not commenced at once—Tell Mr Martin<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a> to let you keep the Pony for your use—Will be glad to see your sketches &amp; letter in the Enterprise<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a>. I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="haven't​ &#xA;    ">havent </a> heard from Mrs Elwell<a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">5</a>—if she got the money—Keep the Mexican saddle for your use <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Don't​ &#xA;    ">Dont</a> bother about going to TE<a name="n06.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n06.note" class="ref">6</a>— to measure the land—till you are there some time. Do you think water can be taken out of the river by a head gate and put on the truck—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Cody
</span>
   </div>
