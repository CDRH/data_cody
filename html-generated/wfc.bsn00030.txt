      <span class="dateline">July 7, 1904.</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

78 Exchange St., 

Buffalo, N. Y.


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:</span>


      <p>We hand you herewith statement showing transactions with respect to Cody property for the six months ending June 24, 1904, showing a net balance of $2452.95 due you as Trustee. Of this amount we have already remitted you $2,000 and now hand you herewith New York exchange, your order, for $452.95, the balance. Please receipt and return the accompanying voucher.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>


         <br/>A.B.Minor<br/>

Sec'y.
