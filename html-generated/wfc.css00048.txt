      <span class="salute">My. Dear. <span class="persName">Sam</span>.</span>



      <p>You are <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="terribly​ ">terriably</a>
    ​ up a stump. and I am <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="really​ ">realy</a>
    ​ sorry for you. You make liberal propositions. but please to understand that I am carrying to many people now, and Hotel 
and Rail roads are expensive. I cant send <span class="persName">Ingraham</span> away as he has arranged his business to go. Sam I will wait a day or Two and see how business is and if I can send for you will do so. But old fellow I fear the Texas Boys would keep you <u>full</u> all the time and

When you come on to New York. when I spoke to about going. if you remember. you said you could go if it would be a help to me I told you then not to go if you did not want to as it was no help to me as I could get plenty of men who wanted to go. You spoke as if it was doing me a great favor to go. You did not appear at all anxious. Then you <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="instead​ ">insted</a>
