      <div class="note">(COPY)</div>

      <span class="dateline">Lincoln, Neb., July 25th, 1900.</span>


Mr. T. E. Calvert,
G. S., Lincoln.

<span class="salute">Dear Sir: </span>


      <p>The following is my estimate of cost of line Toluca to Cody:</p>

      <table>
         <tr>
            <td>Grading,</td>
            <td>$820,153.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Bridging,</td>
            <td>203,830.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Superstructure,</td>
            <td>741,590.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Ballasting,</td>
            <td>212,250.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Fence,</td>
            <td>46,200.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Telegraph,</td>
            <td>26,300.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Right-of-way,</td>
            <td>10,000.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Building,</td>
            <td>67,950.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Total, 131.5 miles,</td>
            <td>$2,128,273.00</td>
         </tr>
         <tr>
            <td>Cost per mile,</td>
            <td>$16,200.00</td>
         </tr>
      </table>

      <p>I have used the unit prices we are doing the work for now on sections opened, and have put a reasonable unit price on the balance.</p>

      <p>I have used $25.00 per ton for steel and 51¢ per tie.  This makes cost per mile 66# steel $5,000.  56# $4,500.</p>

      <p>The $250.000.00 now appropriated will grade;</p>
      <ul>
         <li>20 miles out of Toluca now under contract.</li>
         <li>7 “ at Agency.  Indian work.</li>
         <li>14 “ from Corbett East.  Mormon work.</li>
      </ul>
      <p>It may go further if we do not encounter too much classified material.</p>

      <p>The distance Toluca to Cody - - - - - - - 131.5 miles.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>
         <span class="salute">(Sgd.) I. S. P. Weeks.</span>
      </span>
