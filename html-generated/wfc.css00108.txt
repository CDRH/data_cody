      <span class="salute">My. Dear— Hinkle</span>


      <p>Your favor here <a rel="tooltip" class="abbr" title="received​ &#xA;    ">recd</a>​
— I note what you say about— the— Cody &amp; Salsbury Segregation— I dont think Beck— cares much for the School Section. or you would have heard from him—</p>

      <p>F. C. Kilsey. the Civil Enginer reccomended by Mr— Elwood Meade is makeing a complete survey of the Cody &amp; Salsbury Canal When he is done work will be carried on—</p>

      <p>I appreciate your interest and wont for get you—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>
