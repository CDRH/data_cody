      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>


      <span class="dateline">Chicago<a href="#n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
         <br/>

July 28<a href="#n02" class="internal_link">2</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>
      </span>

      <p>Those Preists<a href="#n03" class="internal_link">3</a> want their deeds to their Lots— I wish you would let me know if you are now ready and will make them out for them?</p>

      <p>Snyder<a href="#n04" class="internal_link">4</a> writes me you have gone South to work for <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span>— not but what I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="approve​ &#xA;    ">approove</a> of it. But I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> 
         <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="believe​ &#xA;    ">beleive</a> that you would leave until the water was again <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="running​ &#xA;    ">runing</a>. If you have left our business just at this critical time— Its not right And I will so notify the Co. I hope <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="it's​ &#xA;    ">its</a> not true—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>
