      <p>Bob Haslam<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a> showed me your letters poor fellow he is in a bad way paralized and broke. I am helping him but I have so many to help— its quite a draw on me.</p>

      <p>President Taft<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a> enjoyed the show. And sent me a nice letter.</p>

      <p>Hope you are all well</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">love to family</span>


         <br/>Col</span>
