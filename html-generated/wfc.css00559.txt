      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">George</span>
      </span>


      <p>Bob Haslam<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> showed me your letters poor fellow he is in a bad way paralized and broke. I am helping him but I have so many to help— its quite a draw on me.</p>

      <p>President Taft<a href="../doc/n04" class="internal_link">4</a> enjoyed the show. And sent me a nice letter.</p>

      <p>Hope you are all well</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">love to family</span>


         <br/>Col</span>

   </div>




   <div class="note">Note 1:  Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined with Pawnee Bill's Great Far East performed in Baltimore, Maryland, April 19-20, 1911. </div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: The year is not inscribed by Cody but is 1911.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: Robert "Pony Bob" Haslam (1840-1912), was born in London, England, and died in Chicago, Illinois; Haslam is famous for having made the longest trip in Pony Express history: 120 miles in eight hours and twenty minutes. Haslam died at the age of 72 in poverty after suffering a stroke.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 4: President Howard Taft (1857-1930).</div>
