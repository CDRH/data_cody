      <p>In yours of 23d you say you sent me a statement. If you did I have not recd it. I am to meet the Buffalo people<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a> in Chicago And we are going to come to an understanding. We <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> go on this way. None of us are satisfied. And we <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> find out what is contracted to be done what has been or where the money has gone. <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span> has been sent for also <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/mead.e">Mead</a>
         </span>. The Buffalo parties ordered this meeting and informed me to day this haphazard way of doing business <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> do. There has got to be some system which does not exist now. I feel sorry for Flood <a name="n03.ref"> </a>
