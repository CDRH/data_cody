      <span class="salute">My. Dear, Hinkle—</span>


      <p>I will probaly leave North Platte for Cody the 9th or 10th When I get to Cody I go on a two weeks hunt.<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> would be delighted to have you as my guest</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>

      </p>
      <span class="closer">
         <br/>W.F.C

   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Columbia, South Carolina, on October 26, 1901.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: Buffalo Bill's Wild West gave its last performance of the 1901 season on October 28.  William F. Cody often came "home" to the Big Horn Basin for hunting trips at the conclusion of the show season.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: Thomas T. Tynan (1860-1925) was Wyoming's superintendent of public instruction from 1899 to 1907.  The superintendent of public instruction served as a member of the state board of land commissioners.  Cody's rent for lands leased from the state would be payable to the board.  Tynan later served as mayor of Sheridan, Wyoming, from 1914 to 1915.</div>
