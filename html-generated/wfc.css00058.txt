      <span class="salute">My Dear. Old Pard. Jim</span>



      <p>Thanks for your letter. I have only time to say that I close here the 22.nd My winter success has been my greatest victory yet I could stay here in New York another Year. if I wished every seat in this immense building is now sold for the rest of the time I am here. &amp; I have got the money in a Bank— Jim old Boy if my health holds out three years
