      <span class="salute">My Dear Friends<br/>
In Rochester</span>


      <p>I have just returned from a big hunt. The Indians in this country are on the war path. And I have just accepted a. commission from the government as Chief of Scouts &amp; Guide for the Expedition that is to be sent in to the Big horn country. The command will number nearly as many men as Gen Custers and be commanded by Col Mills we are going in to one of the worst Indian <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="countries​ ">countrys</a>
