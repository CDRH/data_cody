      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined With Pawnee Bill's Great Far East.</div>

      <span class="dateline">
Zanesville, O.
Jun. 29th<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
      </span>

      <span class="salute">The Elwells<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a>
         <br/>
My Very Dear Friends</span>



      <p>So glad to get your letters and know that you are well and prosperous. But still the Call of the Wild is in your hearts. How I long for the Mountains and sage brush. I cannot be truly happy away from there. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Maybe​ &#xA;    ">Maby</a> we may meet out in our glorious Mountains again. I am well.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Love to all</span>
         <br/>Col
</span>
