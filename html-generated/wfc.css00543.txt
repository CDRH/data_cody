      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>—</span>

      <p>Yours received Am glad you can keep the water coming. What have you done with <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span> 
         <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/salsbury.n">Salsbury</a>
         </span> says for you as Manager to demand a settlement. tell him to send in his bill for salary— &amp; you refer it to the company. if he <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> do it make him do it by law.</p>

      <p>
         <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/belistein.g">Bleistein</a>
         </span> will remit I guess at once. Wrote Martin<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a> to pay my water rights assessment. Mrs Wetmore<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a> will send her payment on land

soon. Kelsey<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a> writes me he has sent maps &amp; report of survey— I presume you have let <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/wiley.s">Wiley</a>
         </span>
         <a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">[5]</a> use them as agreed.</p>

      <p>The Wild West closed big engagement here open Brooklyn tomorrow sent route—</p>

      <p>The Gold Mine<a name="n06.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n06.note" class="ref">[6]</a> is a winner I guess beyond doubt its being kept quiet just now as we want some property Adjoining it and a water right from a stream near by. We are getting out ore and the vein <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="prove​ &#xA;    ">proove</a> a true fissure</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>
