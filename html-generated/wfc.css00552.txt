      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>


      <span class="dateline">Metz Germany<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
         <br/>


Sep. 1st 1906</span>


      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>.</span>


      <p>If nothing happens to prevent I will leave Wapiti<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a> Oct 15th for a ten days hunt. I propose going up Elk fork get up on the Wapiti Ridge<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a> and hunting the heads of Hard Pan<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>. Bob Cat on the south heds of Fish hawk<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>. Eagle<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a> and up wapiti ridge to head of Ishawooa<a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">5</a> &amp; down to TE<a name="n06.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n06.note" class="ref">6</a>. I will have but three or four particular friends with me. And I want you to be one of them I will send a Pack outfit to meet us at Wapiti starting from there Oct 15<u>th</u> I hope to arrive in Cody Oct 10<u>th</u>
      </p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Your old friend</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>


      <p>PS. I hope the Citizens of Cody are urgeing the building of the high line Canal which I have written about before</p>

      <p>Germany is doing fine about $50000 a week-</p>


      <p>Bill</p>
