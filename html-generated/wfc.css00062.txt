New York,

June 12 1888
</span>

      <span class="salute">My. Dear Wheeler</span>



      <p>Thanks for letter to read— In the death of Capt Price the Army has lost one of its best Officers he was a true friend an honest man. and a brave Soldier. I regret his death very much— I hope you will have a Jolly time abroad—</p>

      <p>Enclosed find Card, try &amp; come down &amp; see me—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours Truly</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
