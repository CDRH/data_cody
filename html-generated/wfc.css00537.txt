      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>

      <p>Your favor of Aug 31 pleases me— Yes we must surely have a directors<a href="../doc/" class="internal_link">3</a> meeting and see if we <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ get a <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="move​ ">moove</a>
    ​ on them— I wish you would be prepared with some estimates and suggestions, George keep the water coming till it freezes up— I wrote <span class="persName">Schwoob</span> Cody Trading Co. Should build a big cistern— or the Town<a href="../doc/n05" class="internal_link">5</a> should chip in and build a big one— See if you <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ get them at it— Keep me posted about the hotel<a href="../doc/n06" class="internal_link">6</a>. I hope it will go through.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours</span>  

         <br/>Cody
