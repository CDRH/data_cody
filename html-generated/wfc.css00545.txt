      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>




      <span class="dateline">Sandusky— O.<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">[1]</a>
         <br/>

July- 8<u>th</u> 1902</span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>.</span>


      <p>I am going to again. As I am pushed by <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/salsbury.n">Salsbury</a>
         </span> &amp; <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/bleistein.g">Bleistein</a>
         </span> to hurry things— To see if I can hear from you— here is a <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="telegram​ &#xA;    ">telegrame</a> that explains what the men want done who puts up the money— May I hear what you are doing— toward getting an Estimate. So we can get bids for the work. the Summer is slipping away wire me at my expense so I can reply to Salsbury &amp; Buffalo parties<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a>— I can see no reason why something <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> be done &amp; soon.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>
