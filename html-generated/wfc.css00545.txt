      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>




      <span class="dateline">Sandusky— O.<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
         <br/>

July- 8<u>th</u> 1902</span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>.</span>


      <p>I am going to again. As I am pushed by <span class="persName">Salsbury</span> &amp; <span class="persName">Bleistein</span> to hurry things— To see if I can hear from you— here is a <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="telegram​ ">telegrame</a>
    ​ that explains what the men want done who puts up the money— May I hear what you are doing— toward getting an Estimate. So we can get bids for the work. the Summer is slipping away wire me at my expense so I can reply to Salsbury &amp; Buffalo parties<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a>— I can see no reason why something <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ be done &amp; soon.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>

   </div>




   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Sandusky, Ohio, on July 8, 1902.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "Buffalo parties" are the directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Company, including <span class="persName">Rumsey</span>, Bleistein, and <span class="persName">Gerrans</span>, all from Buffalo, N.Y.</div>
