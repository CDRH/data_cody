                <p>The film rents from $50.00 per day up and when played on a percentage basis from 50 per cent. to 75 per cent. of the gross receipts is the share received by the owners of State Rights.</p>
                <p>Advance the prices for these pictures to 25, 35, and 50 cents.</p>
                <p>If you have any doubts regarding the quality and drawing power of these films, get in touch with any owner of State Rights and learn what they have to say.</p>
