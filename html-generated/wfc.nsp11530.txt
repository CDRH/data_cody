      <p>MARRIAGE OF INDIANS AT OLD TRAFFORD.—The chief <span class="persName">Black Heart</span>, of the Ogallalla tribe of Sioux will be married this morning at St. Bride's Church, Old Trafford. The bride, of the same race as the bridegroom, is Calls-the-Name, sister of the chief <span class="persName">No Neck</span>, who will "give her away." Mr. <span class="persName">J. C. Crager</span>, Buffalo Bill's interpreter, is to be the best man. All the parties mentioned aremembers of Col. W. F. Cody's troupe, and after the marriage ceremony, which is to be witnessed by the Indians and whites of the Wild West, including the Cowboy Band, there is to be a nuptial feast in the camp at Whalley Range.</p>
