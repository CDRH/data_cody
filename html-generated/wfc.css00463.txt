      <span class="dateline">Atlanta<a name="n01.ref"> </a>

      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Geo</a>
         </span>
      </span>

      <p>I close here Saturday Nov 2d<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>— <u>Busted</u>. This is the worst place we have struck no one coming to this Fair. You must close down fast or if I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> get new blood<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">4</a> in we will be in an awful tight place— Do you appreciate what I mea[n] I will do my part but get down as easy on me as you possibly can this I have written you several times.</p>

      <p>I will give you my route as near as I can.  <del>wire</del> Nov. 6 &amp; 7. Continental Hotel Philadelphia Pa— <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/salsbury.n">Salsburys</a>
         </span> Address till Nov 16th Grand Opera House Philadelphia Pa Better direct there. I will try and be with <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/bleistein.g">Geo Bleistein</a>
         </span>. Buffalo. Oct 9th then we start for you<a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">5</a>—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Bill
</span>
