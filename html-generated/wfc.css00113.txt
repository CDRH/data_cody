      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>

      <span class="dateline">Mattoon Ill<br/>

Sep- 25th 01
</span>

      <span class="salute">My. Dear. Kelsey.</span>


      <p>Your favor 19th You had better get your rail road transportation extended good for Nov— If you get to Hoffman House New—York Nov 2nd it will be about right— I think you had better keep all maps &amp; c— And bring them with you— And have other maps of Big Horn county and Burlington rail road maps— So we can show our location clearly.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>
