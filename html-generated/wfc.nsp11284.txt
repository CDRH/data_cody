         <strong>McCune as Office Manager</strong> — Major William F. McCune, for many years with the Buffalo Bill Wild West show, will have charge of the state headquarters of Fred H. Garlow, who has the state right for the Buffalo Bill Indian war pictures.  The offices are in room 436 Bee building.  The pictures will be at the American theater for the remainder of the week, when they will be shown at South Omaha for half a week.</p>
