      <span class="dateline">July 2nd 1904</span>

      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey,</span>

Buffalo, N. Y.


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:-</span>


      <p>Enclosed please find cancelled note of $10,000 given by the Cody Trading Company.</p>

      <p>We will keep the demand note and use that in the future and have endorsed the interest on same to January 4th, 1905.</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Respectfully Yours,</span>

         <br/>Louis F. McLean<br/>
