            <h3>Illustrations</h3>
            <p> Comprised of mass-produced book illustrations, engravings, and cartoons of William F. "Buffalo Bill" Cody, <i>Buffalo
                Bill's Wild West</i> performers, and scenes related to Cody's life and experiences.</p>
        </a>
        
        <a href="visual_art/" class="subCategory">
            <div class="image">
            <img src="../images/icon-paintings.jpg"/>
         </div>
            <h3>Visual Art</h3>
            <p>Works of visual art that either have William F. "Buffalo Bill" Cody as a subject, or pieces that Cody commissioned himself as a patron of the arts.</p>
        </a>
        
        <a href="photographs/" class="subCategory">
            <div class="image">
            <img src="../images/icon-photographs.jpg"/>
         </div>
            <h3>Photographs</h3>
            <p>A vast collection of photographic images that include portraits of Cody and his family, <em>Buffalo Bill's Wild West</em> performers, and scenes from places Cody lived and visited.</p>
        </a>
        
        <a href="posters/" class="subCategory">
            <div class="image">
            <img src="../images/icon-posters.jpg"/>
         </div>
            <h3>Posters</h3>
            <p>Promotional materials and works of popular art that include monochromatic posters and multicolored lithographs featuring Cody and <em>Buffalo Bill's Wild West</em> exhibitions.</p>
        </a>
        
        <a href="postcards/" class="subCategory">
            <div class="image">
            <img src="../images/icon-postcards.jpg"/>
         </div>
            <h3>Postcards</h3>
            <p>Mementos of <em>Buffalo Bill's Wild West</em> tours and brief, informal correspondence to and from William F. "Buffalo Bill" Cody.</p>
        </a>
        
        <a href="cabinet_cards/" class="subCategory">
            <div class="image">
            <img src="../images/icon-cabinet_cards.jpg"/>
         </div>
            <h3>Cabinet Cards</h3>
            <p>Commercially-produced carte de visite, cabinet cards, and other such photograph cards of photographic portraiture intended for collection.</p>
        </a>
