June. 2<u>d</u> 99</span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>
      </span>


      <p>Every day now every mail every telegrame Boy I see- I say there is water running in the streets of Cody.</p>

      <p>Say. I think we should fix the road through to Frosts<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> I understand it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ ">wont</a>
    ​ cost over $25—</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours in haste</span>


         <br/>Cody</span>


   </div>




   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, from May 29 to June 3, 1899.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "Frosts" may refer to property owned by Mahlon Frost (1839-1929), located on Sage Creek east of Cody, Wyoming. Cody is suggesting that road grading be completed to the edge of that property.</div>
