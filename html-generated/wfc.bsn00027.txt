      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

78 Exchange St.,

Buffalo, N. Y.


<p>Dear Sir:- We hand you herewith statement of the business transacted with respect to the Cody property for the six months ending Dec. 31, 1902, which shows a net balance from this property of $1277.59, for which amount we hand you herewith draft on the Chemical National Bank of New York. Be kind enough to sign, date and return the accompanying voucher.</p>

      <p>We trust you will find the statement satisfactory</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>

         <br/>ABMinor<br/>

Sec'y
