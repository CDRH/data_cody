      <span class="dateline">Sunday morning nearing Atlanta— on train—<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">George</span>
      </span>


      <p>I wrote you last night. Since then I have put in a bad night looking over your letter. You say 62 teams &amp; a 103 men will cost up to Dec. 1st from Oct 21 will cost 8.910. if it does they are <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="making​ ">makeing</a>
    ​ big wages I tell you. I figure they work 30 days in that time. 62 teams and men at $300 a day for team &amp; man and 1.50 a day for 40 men extra is <del>7</del> $7,380. I have no idea what your flumes will cost.</p>

      <p>
         <span class="persName">Alger</span> was with you about the time you wrote this letter His letter &amp; yours so wide apart Did you &amp; he talk it over together. If not what did he go up there for? George I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ ">dont</a>
