      <p>As the time grows near for My going to the Basin— I feel like letting you know that I hope we may meet as friends. I admit that at times I have felt hurt at the annoyances of our troubles there. But I know that you have had a hard time to do with nothing As our stock holders have fallen down entirely. And you and I have had to stand the brunt. And I expect we will still have to do it. As <span class="persName">Bleistein</span> writes me that the Buffalo parties<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> wish to sell out I am perfectly willing that they should.
