      <span class="dateline">Auburn. Aug 8th<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
      </span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>
      </span>

      <p>Yours of 4th in it you do not say anything what is best to do— or where I am to write you. Or who is in the new Co—<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> So I have nothing to say— as I know nothing— probably <span class="persName">Alger</span> will write I wrote Alger that <span class="persName">Mead</span> would be there the 18th with a party of men from <del>pl</del> Cincinnati O. who may want to go in with us—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly your friend </span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>

   <div class="note">Note 1: The year is not inscribed by Cody but is 1895 as determined by the Routes List: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Auburn, New York, on August 8, 1895.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: Cody's reference to "the new Co" was likely to Shoshone Land and Irrigation Company which later became Shoshone Irrigation Company.</div>
