      <p>Yours of 4th in it you do not say anything what is best to do— or where I am to write you. Or who is in the new Co—<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a> So I have nothing to say— as I know nothing— probably <span class="persName">

            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/bmead.e">Mead</a>
         </span> would be there the 18th with a party of men from <del>pl</del> Cincinnati O. who may want to go in with us—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly your friend </span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
