      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>

      <span class="dateline">Big. Rapids. Mich<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
         <br/>

July. 20<u>th</u> 98</span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>—</span>

      <p>Your letter of the 13th sent Manistee<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a> forwarded here. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="It's​ &#xA;    ">Its</a> too bad about the Dam washing out, as Gen. Manager. you should bar cloud bursts. Well it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> be helped. And you done perfectly right In repairing the damage and getting water to the farms and Cody. As soon as possible. I hope you have pushed the work. I know the importance of getting the water quick. I will send <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span> $200. and will ask <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/salsbury.n">Salsbury</a>
         </span> to do the same. Also the other stock holders<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>. Keep me posted—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="hastily​ &#xA;    ">hastly</a>
         </span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>

      <p>P. S. I fear the mill will have to hold over <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="another​ &#xA;    ">annother</a> year—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <br/>W. F. C</span>
