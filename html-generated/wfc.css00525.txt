      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>

      <span class="dateline">Big. Rapids. Mich<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
         <br/>

July. 20<u>th</u> 98</span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>—</span>

      <p>Your letter of the 13th sent Manistee<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> forwarded here. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="It's​ ">Its</a>
    ​ too bad about the Dam washing out, as Gen. Manager. you should bar cloud bursts. Well it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ be helped. And you done perfectly right In repairing the damage and getting water to the farms and Cody. As soon as possible. I hope you have pushed the work. I know the importance of getting the water quick. I will send <span class="persName">Alger</span> $200. and will ask <span class="persName">Salsbury</span> to do the same. Also the other stock holders<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a>. Keep me posted—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="hastily​ ">hastly</a>
    ​</span>

         <br/>W. F. Cody
</span>

      <p>P. S. I fear the mill will have to hold over <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="another​ ">annother</a>
    ​ year—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <br/>W. F. C</span>

   </div>



   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Big Rapids, Michigan, on July 20, 1898. </div> 

   <div class="note">Note 2: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Manistee, Michigan, on July 18, 1898.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: "Stock holders" are the directors and investors of Shoshone Irrigation Company.</div>
