      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">George</span>—</span>

      <p>I was in Chicago three days— The Colony<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> is to leave there the 15th I fixed up a contract with Nagle Bro.<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> which I have shown to our Co here— they seem to think Nagles should not take out their whole commission the first payment of lands but said to let it go— As Nagle<a href="../doc/n04" class="internal_link">4</a> is to give a bond of $10,000. But Nagle said he was agreeable to leave his commissions up to $10,000 in our hands as a guarantee of good faith. He wanted $300 per month for office &amp; advertiseing expenses— I suggested 250— our friends here think

      <p>I told him to make out his bill. that it would then go before a regular meeting &amp; if <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="approved​ ">approoved</a>

   <div class="note">Note 1: On Monday, April 6, 1896, Buffalo Bill's Wild West began preparations for the opening of the 1896 season in Philadelphia on Saturday, April 18th.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "The Colony" refers to the group of fifty or so German migrant families who chose to settle in the Big Horn Basin because of promotions made by S. V. Nagle, an agent of the Chicago firm of F. A. Nagle Commission Merchants.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: "Nagle Bro." refers to F. A. Nagle and S. V. Nagle of the Chicago firm of F. A. Nagle Commission Merchants, who attempted to recruit settlers to the lands to be irrigated by the Cody Canal.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 4: S. V. Nagle, agent of the Chicago firm of F. A. Nagle Commission Merchants, was the primary agent for the German migrant families coming to settle in the Big Horn Basin.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 5: "Placer Fields" may refer to one of several mineral claims filed by Beck around the Cody area on behalf of Cody and other members of Shoshone Irrigation Company.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 6: "Stump" or "Stumps" is not identified.</div>
