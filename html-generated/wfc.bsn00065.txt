      <span class="dateline">August 4, 1913</span>


H.M. Gerrans,

Hotel Iroquois, Buffalo, N. Y.


<p>Will arrange to take up draft in about one week  Delayed replying thinking might be able to do so at once but hardly possible to do this  If draft is a cash item can you notify your bank that we would like a few days time. Can send check at once for one thousand.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <br/>J.M. Schwoob.

P A I D</span>
