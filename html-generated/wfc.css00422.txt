      <span class="dateline">New York<br/>
May 11th</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Tait.</span>


      <p>Yours of may 4th came yesterday. The show is over in Jersey City.</p>


      <p>
         <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="I'll​ ">Ill</a>
    ​ go over in the morning early as I now I make parades in my spider. We done well here. But this as you know is an expensive City.</p>

      <p>Now Tait do all you can for Mr Smith the Expert for so much depends on a favorable report from him.</p>

      <p>I hope he will trace the Scheelite from Morning Star to Maudina. I want him to examine the low grade gold place Black struck near the end on the Star. And <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="in fact​ ">infact</a>
    ​ everything. Knoblauch is no good.</p>

      <p>If <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Smith's​ ">Smiths</a>
    ​ report is favorable you and a whole lot

         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="large/wfc.css00422.02.jpg"

of men will be put on salaries. And lively times at the mines.</p>

      <p>So do your level best.</p>

      <p>Of course Smith <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ ">wont</a>
    ​ say what he thinks. But will make his report to the men who sent him. But your natural instinct. And being a good deal with him of nights you can form some idea if he likes the proposition.</p>

      <p>And let me know</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours in haste</span>

         <br/>Gov
