North. Platte.
July. 5th 79
</span>

      <span class="salute">My. Dear. Sam.</span>


      <p>Just came in from branding calves. tired out but feeling bully otherwise. <span class="unclear illegible">[taned?]</span> to beat h— Sorry you were feeling so blue. and if I was not so hard pushed for scads I would just send you a hundred for luck. but I am pushed to the wall until I can ship beeves or get to work. if I were not going away so soon would have you to come out. but I will make a brake next week first to Colorado then to N.Y. back to Davenport. Iowa where I open Sep. 1st. in regard to you going on the boards again. I must think of it I have not part in either of my dramas that would be suitable for you to play. as I did say that I would never again have another

Scout or western man with me that is one <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="whom​ ">whoom</a>
    ​ I would work up. for just as soon as they see their names in print a few times they git the big head. and want to start a company of their own. I will name <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="a few​ ">afew</a>
    ​. Wild Bill. Texas Jack. John Nelson. Oregon Bill. Kit Carson.Capt Jack &amp;c. all busted flat before they were out a month. and wanted to come back because I would not take them then they talked about me. But old Pard I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ ">dont</a>
    ​ <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="really​ ">realy</a>
    ​ believe you are that kind of a hair pin. And I will see what can be done. I see in a Chicago paper the other day. where Kit Carson.(Jr.) was arrested for <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="striking​ ">strikeing</a>
    ​ his wife with intent to kill. was bound over to the next court. I expected as much. when he married that old dame. Kit by the way <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="ain't​ ">aint</a>
    ​ a bad fellow. but he thought at one time that he was a little K Christ. He is out of luck. had better stuck to the old man. run out of paper good by—</p>



      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Cody

<span class="salute">With Kind wishes</span>
