      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined With Pawnee Bill's Great Far East.</div>



      <span class="dateline">Philadelphia<a href="#n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
         <br/>


May 27<u>th</u> / 09</span>


      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>.</span>


      <p>I am feeling better thank you. I have gotten rid of the Bailey Estate.<a href="#n02" class="internal_link">2</a> 
         <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/lillie.g">Lillie</a>
         </span> and I now own everything. 70 rail road cars. 170 big draft horses. Elephants <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="camels​ &#xA;    ">cammels</a> in fact the Entire outfit valued at $300,000 of course it will take me a few weeks to pay some money I had to borrow. but six weeks good biz and I am clear. we will take $60,000 here this week. Lillie is a close conservative manager <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="always​ &#xA;    ">allways</a> on the job. And it <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> be long now until I am on easy avenue again.</p>

      <p>The last few years have been hard ones for me but I never weakened— and my good friends have stood by me. And I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ &#xA;    ">wont</a> for get them. And one of them is the First National of Cody.<a href="#n03" class="internal_link">3</a>
      </p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Put that in your pipe</span>


         <br/>Col</span>
