   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink" href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/terry.e">Terry, Ellen, Dame, 1847-1928</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">Alice Ellen Terry (1847-1928), an English stage actress, was among the most famous leading ladies of the Victorian era. She received an honorary degree from the University of St. Andrews in 1922 and in 1925 was made a Dame, Grand Cross, of the Order of the British Empire.</div>
