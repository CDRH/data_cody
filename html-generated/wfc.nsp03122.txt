      <h3>Fight Between the Forces of Buffalo Bill and Dr. Carver.</h3>

      <span class="gap sampling">[sampling]</span>

      <p>LONDON, Aug 25.â€”Meagre details have just been received here of a fight between the cowboy and Indian companies of Buffalo Bill and <span class="persName">Dr. Carver</span> in the streets of Hamburg.<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a> It is understood that a fierce conflict took place, and that but for the interference of the police in force life would have been taken. Nothing else is talked of.</p>

      <p>Dr. Carver's company is but lately away from Ruseia, where in Moscow it had great trouble with the police, when blood was shed.</p>

      <p>For a long time there had been great rivalry between Dr. Carver and Buffalo Bill. The latter opened his show in Hamburg yesterday.</p>
