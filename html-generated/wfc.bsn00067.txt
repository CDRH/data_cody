      <span class="dateline">August 6, 1913</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. H. M. Gerrans</span>,

Hotel Iroquois, 

Buffalo, N. Y.


<span class="salute">Dear Mr. Gerrans;</span>


      <p>Your telegram, instructing us to draw on <span class="persName">Mr. Bleistein</span> for $1500.00, at hand. We have drawn this morning, making a cash item of it. The $2500.00 draft which Mr. Bleistein made on us has not <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="been presented​ ">beenpresented</a>
    ​, or has not reached Cody yet, but we presume it will be within a day or two and we will take care of it.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours,</span>
