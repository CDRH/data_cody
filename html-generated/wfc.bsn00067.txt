            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/bleistein.g">Mr. Bleistein</a>
         </span> for $1500.00, at hand. We have drawn this morning, making a cash item of it. The $2500.00 draft which Mr. Bleistein made on us has not <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="been presented​ &#xA;    ">beenpresented</a>, or has not reached Cody yet, but we presume it will be within a day or two and we will take care of it.</p>
