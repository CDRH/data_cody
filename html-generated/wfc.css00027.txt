      <span class="salute">Buck</span>


      <p>My Children at Cody are getting up an illustrated folder of my dear old Home Ranch— No man ever looked on a prettier place. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="It's​ ">Its</a>
    ​ the greatest hunting and fishing. place on earth— I want you to write. Fred H. Garlow. Cody Wyo. To send you some folders—</p>

      <p>And that you can and will send them some Summer guests and will be there your self with a party this fall—</p>

      <p>Now Buck you can do this. And you do it. See! And do it while the trail is hot. Do you get me—</p>

      <p>
         <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="I'll​ ">Ill</a>
    ​ sure show Greensburg with my <span class="unclear ">[own?]</span> Show this Summer. And <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="I'll​ ">Ill</a>
    ​ boom your game—You get busy &amp; do as much for me—</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Bill
