      <span class="dateline">July 9, 1903.</span>


      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee

78 Exchange St.,

Buffalo, N. Y.



<p>Dear Sir:- We hand you herewith the semi-annual statement of business transacted at Cody with respect to the half interest represented by yourself, as Trustee, which shows a net balance due your half interest of $1748.13, voucher and draft for which amount is also enclosed. Please sign and return the voucher, and oblige.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>


         <br/>ABMinor<br/>

Sec'y.
