   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink" href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/heckert.t">Heckert, Theodore</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">Theo or Theodore Heckert was a railroad contractor who owned grading equipment used in land development. In 1895 George Beck, Chief Surveyor Charles Hayden, and Heckert set up a grading camp and started construction of the head gate and flume of the Cody Canal.</div>
