      <h3>Indians for Buffalo Bill.</h3>

      <p>About twenty Indians from Pine Ridge, in charge of Buffalo Bill's representative, arrived here today. They were joined by the Indians now at Fort Sheridan and the party left for the East this afternoon.</p>
