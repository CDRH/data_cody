      <h3>Buffalo Bill in Berlin.</h3>
      <p>BERLIN, July 25&#x97;Ten thousand people, of whom 500 were Americans, attended Buffalo Bill's opening Wednesday. A bucking bull broke loose and rushed toward the frightened people in the cheap seats. Col. Cody lassoed the furious brute amid roars of applause. Among those present were Georgia Cayvan, Jennie June's daughter, Veda Croby, Minister Phelps and the ladies of his family, Mr. and Mrs. Coleman, Consuls General Edwards and Johnson, the Princess Rupprecht of Bavaria, Maximilian of Baden and Leopold of Prussia, together with hundreds of cavalry officers of the highest rank.</p>
