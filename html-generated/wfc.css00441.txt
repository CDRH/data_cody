      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>

      <p>Yours <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="received​ ">recd</a>
    ​. I have been so over worked could not write you as fully &amp; as often as I would like to— <span class="persName">Alger</span> expects to start west the 1st of July. And is in hopes of <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="taking​ ">takeing</a>
    ​ two or three Boston men with him. George we have got to get some one with money into it. If <span class="persName">Paxton</span> and Creighton<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> would go in $50,000 will open up the the 25000 acre tract. after that it will be easy sailing. I can put <span class="persName">Heckerts</span> outfit to work And commence work but I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
