   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink" href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/applin.v">Applin, Vincent Augustin</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">Vincent Augustin Applin (1850-1895) was a member of the Incorporated Law Society and Solicitor of the Supreme Court in England. Applin became secretary of The American Exhibition of 1887, along with fellow appointees Colonel Henry S. Russell (president) and John Robinson Whitley (director-general).</div>
