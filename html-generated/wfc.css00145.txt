      <span class="salute">Dear Julia.</span>


      <p>Yours written on your 60th birth day received. I am pleased that you feel so well. I wish I could say I did. But I am going to get out of this business— that is just wearing the life out of me— there is such a nervous strain <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="continually​ ">continualy</a>
    ​— And the thing has got onto my nerves— And this must be my last summer. to day <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="it's​ ">its</a>
    ​ snowing here. And we open out doors tomorrow

I am going to fix dear old TE. up And go there and rest this winter—I have just got to go someplace where <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="it's​ ">its</a>
    ​ quiet and rest. And get my nerves quiet— I think when Lou and Arta sees I am determined— they will agree to a mutual <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="separation​ ">seporation</a>
