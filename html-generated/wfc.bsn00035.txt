      <span class="persName">Mr. Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

78 Exchange Street,

Buffalo, N. Y.


<span class="salute">Dear Sir:</span>


      <p>By request of Col. Cody, we send you herewith draft on the Chemical National Bank of New York No. 391544 of the First National Bank of this city for $11,000 as advance payment on proceeds of the one-half interest in the Cody property held by you as Trustee. A final accounting with statement <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="and remittance​ ">andremittance</a>
    ​ will be made at the usual time at the close of the year. Be kind enough to receipt and return the accompanying voucher.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>


         <br/>ABMinor<br/>


Sec'y

      <span class="dateline">Jan. 18, 1906.</span>

Mr. Bronson Rumsey,

78 Exchange Street, 

Buffalo, New York.

<span class="salute">Dear Sir:</span>


      <p>I hand you herewith statement of account of the one-half interest in the Cody, Wyoming property represented by you, showing all transactions for the six months ending Dec. 30, 1905.</p>

      <p>The net balance for your half interest for that time is $12679.05, of which we remitted you $11,000.00, on the 14th ult. We now hand you herewith draft to your order for $1679.05, the balance. Please return voucher.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Very truly yours,</span>

         <br/>ABMinor<br/>

Sec'y
