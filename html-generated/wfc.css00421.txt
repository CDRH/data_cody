      <span class="dateline">Washington, D.C., Feb 23, 19__</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Tait.</span>


      <p>Only a line. Thursday evening we show the pictures to the President. Secretaries &amp;c—</p>

      <p>Friday Matinee to Press, Army and others. Monday Mar 2d open at Lyceum theatre New York for real Money. I am to lecture on the pictures first week in New York.</p> 

      <p>If you are broke ask Mr Tammen for a loan.</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">In haste</span>

         <br/>Governor
