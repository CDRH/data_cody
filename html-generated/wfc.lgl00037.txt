      <p>I, Carl S. Milliken, Secretary of State of the State of Colorado, do hereby certify that
-- in accordance with the records of this office --</p>

      <p>THE COL. W. F. CODY ("BUFFALO BILL") HISTORICAL PICTURES COMPANY</p>

      <p>a DOMESTIC CORPORATION existing under and by virtue of the Laws of the STATE OF COLORADO and duly authorized to transact business in the STATE OF COLORADO, has failed to file an ANNUAL REPORT in this office for the years 1918, 1919, 1920, and 1921, as by Law provided.</p>
