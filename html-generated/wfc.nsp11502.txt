      <p>
         <span class="persName">Otakte</span>, or "Kill Many," one of Buffalo Bill's Sioux, who returned from Europe on Saturday seriously injured by a fall from his horse while with the show in Berlin, died in Bellevue Hospital on Tuesday. His body will be taken to the Sioux reservation by "Hank" Clifford<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a> and <span class="persName">George C. Crager</span>, who were with Buffalo Bill as interpreters.</p>
