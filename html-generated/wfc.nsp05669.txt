                <h3>WILD WEST NOW IN THE MOVIES</h3>
                <h3>MOVING PICTURE MEN BUY BUFFALO BILL'S STAGE COACHES, BRONCHOS, ETC.</h3>
                <span class="ab">By United Press</span>
                <p>Denver, Aug. 22 — Moving picture men today prepared to move the bulk of Buffalo Bill's Wild West show out of the city, after purchasing it at sheriff's sale.  The auctioneer disposed of $6,800 worth of bucking bronchos, stage coaches and trained animals.  Colonel C. J. Bills of Lincoln, Neb., bought Buffalo Bill's horse Isham, for $150 and will present it to Colonel Cody.</p>
