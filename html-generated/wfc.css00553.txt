      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>



      <span class="dateline">Gladback<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">[1]</a>
         <br/>

Sep. 8th / 06</span>


      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>—</span>

 
      <p>Your letter came to day And as Geo. B. Harris<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a> is so anxious to know what the people of Cody &amp; my self are doing about the high line canal— I took the liberty of sending him your letter.</p>

      <p>Well George— <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Yesterday​ &#xA;    ">Yestarday</a> Mr Geo. O. Starr<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a> Presedent of the Barnum &amp; Bailey Ltd. Co. came over from America to see me— About showing in America. And I signed the best show contract for Little Willie<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a> that he has ever had— And the first good one I have ever had. Mr Starr left soon as the contract was signed for America to commence building an entire new outfit for me— 60 new cars— &amp;c. I got L<a name="n05.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n05.note" class="ref">[5]</a> in Hungary &amp; Poland but have <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="pulled​ &#xA;    ">puled</a> out in Germany.</p>

      <p>
         <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="I'll​ &#xA;    ">Ill</a> tell you all about it in the Rockies when we make our ten days hunt</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Love to family. Kiss Betty<a name="n06.ref"> </a>
            <a href="#n06.note" class="ref">[6]</a> for me</span>


         <br/>Col</span>

   </div>


   <div class="bibliography">
      <p>
         <a name="n01.note"> </a>Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed at Monchengladbach, Germany, on September 8, 1906. Cody abbreviates the German city as "Gladback." <a href="#n01.ref"> [back]</a>
      </p>
      <p>
         <a name="n02.note"> </a>Note 2: George B. Harris (1866-1918), second vice-president of the C. B. &amp; Q. Railroad, became president of the company in 1901 until his resignation in 1910.<a href="#n02.ref"> [back]</a>
      </p>
      <p>
         <a name="n03.note"> </a>Note 3: George O. Starr (1849-1915) began his show career in a dime museum in the Bowery, becoming the press agent for P. T. Barnum's circus in 1878; by 1905 he was general manager for Barnum &amp; Bailey.<a href="#n03.ref"> [back]</a>
      </p>
      <p>
         <a name="n04.note"> </a>Note 4: "Little Willie" is Cody's reference to himself.<a href="#n04.ref"> [back]</a>
      </p>

      </p>
