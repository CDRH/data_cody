      <span class="salute">My. Dear. Mollie</span>


      <p>I will arrive in St Louis May 3th. Now I think it will be just as well for you to arrive there about the same time go to the St James Hotel if I dont Meet you at the Depot. and let me know you are there I hope you are well and happy. I have the white horse for you—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Love</span>

         <br/>W. F. C.
