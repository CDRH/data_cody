      <span class="salute">My Dear Sister</span>


      <p>You no doubt will be surprised to hear from me away up in this country. I presume you heard I had gone back to my old life that of a Scout. Well so I did I could not remain East. While this Indian

<span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="large/wfc.css00040.002.jpg"
               rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]"

war was going on without <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="taking​ ">takeing</a>
    ​ a hand. I went to Cheyenne the first of June. I would have come by and seen you. but the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Government​ ">Goverment</a>
    ​ was hurrying me up so I could not find time. I did not have time to go to Denver and see Nellie &amp; May. So Ed brought them over to Cheyenne. that since then I have been <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="continually​ ">continualy</a>
    ​ in the saddle I have been in several fights. and killed three Indians 



during the summer. I call my own. That is I never say that I kill an Indian without I get his scalp. I sent two scalps east to the Sentennial and have one on the boat with me that I killed one week ago <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="yesterday​ ">yestarday</a>
    ​. But it came near being my last. for the indians had the first shot and tore a small piece out of my side. but it was his last shot. I am now carrying his scalp home

My wound is but a slight one. two weeks at home will fix me all right. then I expect to return to my command I was Chief of Scouts for General Crook. But got <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="separated​ ">seporated</a>
    ​ from him in carrying dispatches to General Terry and got wounded on my way to Gen Terry. and could not get back to Crook I have no time to write you all so good bye will write you from home. Love to all from your Brother</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Will
