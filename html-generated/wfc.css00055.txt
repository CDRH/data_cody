      <div class="note">No 5</div>


      <span class="dateline">
March 1st
</span>

      <span class="salute">My Dear. Little Favorite,</span>



      <p>Your very kind &amp; ever interesting letter <a rel="tooltip" class="abbr" title="received​ ">recd</a>​ Now <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ ">dont</a>
    ​ fear about my better half. I will tell you a secret— My better half and I have separated. someday I will tell you all about it. Now do you think any the less of me? I wish I had time to write you a long letter answer all your questions. and tell you of my self &amp;c. but I have not the time &amp; perhaps it might not interest you. I am not very well have a very bad cold. &amp; I have ever so much to do. Yes little Girl Just as soon as I can

<span class="hr"> </span>
         <span class="pageimage">
            <a href="large/wfc.css00055.002.jpg"
               rel="prettyPhoto[pp_gal]"
               title="&lt;a href=&#34;large/wfc.css00055.002.jpg&#34; target=&#34;_blank&#34; &gt;open image in new window&lt;/a&gt;">
               <img src="thumbnails/wfc.css00055.002.jpg" class="display &#xA;            "/>
            </a>
         </span>

I will send you the Locket with picture. I know if I had a dear little some one whom you can guess. to play &amp; sing for me it would drive away the blues— who know but what some day I may have her Eh! I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ ">cant</a>
    ​ say yet for certain if I will go to Europe this spring or not— will keep you posted. Now as you have asked for my picture. I want to do the same. so please send me yours. and without delay. wont You?</p>

      <p>I must now close so many bothering me—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Love &amp; a Kiss to My little Girl</span>
         <br/>from Her Big Boy
</span>
