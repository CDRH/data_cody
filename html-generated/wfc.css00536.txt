      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>


      <span class="dateline">Binghamton. N. Y.<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">[1]</a>
         <br/>


July 18th 99</span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">Beck</a>
         </span>
      </span>

      <p>I was glad to hear that you had been to see the Coal fiends<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a>— <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Don't​ &#xA;    ">Dont</a> you think it a good proposition to look after— Am anxious to hear where the Surveyors are at— Wish you could go down and trace the croppings. And I think we had better do some <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="staking​ &#xA;    ">stakeing</a> and <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="filing​ &#xA;    ">fileing</a>— <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ &#xA;    ">dont</a> you? let me hear from you on the subject— Hope you will all have a nice time on the pleasure trip<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a>— remember Remington<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a> has a mouth and likes his toddy— so stay with him. <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/gerrans.h">Gerrans</a>
         </span> says that whiskey is the best— </p>


      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Cody</span>
