      <p>The Directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Co— wish me to call a meeting to be held in Buffalo the last week in August— And want both your self and <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/alger.h">Alger</a>
         </span> present— with all Books. So please send out a call for any day of that week after Monday the 26<u>th</u> let me know by return mail what date you make for meeting.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody

President.</span>
