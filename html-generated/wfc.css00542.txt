      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>



      <span class="dateline">Newton Ia<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
         <br/>

Aug. 11th 1901</span>


      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>
      </span>


      <p>The Directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Co— wish me to call a meeting to be held in Buffalo the last week in August— And want both your self and <span class="persName">Alger</span> present— with all Books. So please send out a call for any day of that week after Monday the 26<u>th</u> let me know by return mail what date you make for meeting.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody

President.</span>

   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Newton, Iowa, on August 10, 1901. No performance was given on Sunday, August 11, 1901.</div>
