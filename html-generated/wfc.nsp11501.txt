      <h3>"KILLS HIS PONY" NO MORE.</h3>

      <div class="milestone line"> </div>

      <h3>One of Buffalo Bills Indian's Dies in Bellevue Hospital.</h3>

      <p>
         <span class="persName">Otakte</span>, or "Kills His Pony," the Sioux Indian, died in Bellevue Hospital this morning.<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
      </p>

      <p>"Kills His Pony" was a Dacotah, and had been with Buffalo Bill's Wild West Show in Europe. Saturday he arrived in this port on the steamship Salle very ill with consumption.<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a>
      </p>

   </div>




   <div class="note">Note 1: <em class="hi">Otakte,</em> or Kills His Pony, died the night of June 17-18, 1890.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: The cause of Otakte's death may have been a combination of tuberculosis and blood poisoning following a fall from his horse while performing with Buffalo Bill's Wild West in Germany.</div>
