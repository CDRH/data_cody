      <span class="salute">My Dear Boy Michael</span>


      <p>I know you are anxious to hear from me. I would have wrote you before but have been so <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="undecided​ ">undicided</a>
    ​ to know what I am going to do my self. theYellow Fever is so bad I may yet come back to Baltimore and if I do then I will arrange to send you away. if not I will write you in a few days what to do.</p>

      <p>I hope you are all O.K. Write me to. Charleston. S. C. on receipt of this. Give my kind
