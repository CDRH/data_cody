      <span class="dateline">New York, Mar. 23 1902</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Julia.</span>


      <p>I have been thinking over what you said that you have a mortgage on your home. And that you felt like doing something that you did not like North Platte any to well any how. &amp;c- I am going to build a beautiful little hotel in Cody. As you know it's a lovely country the finest climate in the world, its to be my home Now I have got to get some one to run this hotel— for I will not rent it. for I am going to furnish
