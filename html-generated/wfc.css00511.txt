      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g"/>George</span>.</span>

      <p>I am fretting and <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="can't​ &#xA;    ">cant</a> hear from you or in fact from any one from that end. <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="Can't​ &#xA;    ">Cant</a> tell what you are doing. The last I heard from any of you— Paxton &amp; Gallagher<a href="#n03" class="internal_link">3</a> wired me to go security for <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="another​ &#xA;    ">annother</a> bill of goods. Saying $1.306 past due. Buffalo partners<a href="#n04" class="internal_link">4</a> refuse absolutely to do nothing. As I have written time and again— I have no idea what you are doing</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours—</span>


         <br/>Col</span>
