      <p>Please let me know how you are getting on with ditch. When do you expect to have water to town<a href="#n02" class="internal_link">2</a>. How are you <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="making​ &#xA;    ">makeing</a> it with <span class="persName">

      </p>


      <span class="closer">
