      <p>An application was made to Mr. Justice Chitty yesterday for an interim injunction to restrain the proprietors of the American Exhibition from creating a nuisance by noise and smell by Buffalo Bill's Wild West. Affidavits from several of the neighbours, who declared they had not suffered any annoyance or inconvenience, were produced for the defence, and the further hearing was adjourned to next Thursday.</p>
