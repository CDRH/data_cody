      <span class="persName">Bronson Rumsey</span>, Trustee,

78 Exchange St., 

Buffalo, New York.

<span class="salute">Dear Sir:</span>

      <p>We hand you herewith the usual semi-annual statement of business transacted with respect to the Cody, Wyoming, property held jointly by this Company, and a number of persons for whom you act as trustee, for the six months ending June 30, 1907.</p>

      <p>We also enclose draft, your order, for $718.57, for one-half of the net proceeds due you.</p>

      <p>Please receipt and return the accompanying voucher.</p>

      <p>I trust you will find the statement satisfactory.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly,</span>

         <br/>ABMinor<br/>

Secy.
