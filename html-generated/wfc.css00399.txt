      <span class="dateline">Chicago July. 6 191_<br/>
Sunday</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear, Tait.</span>



      <p>I arrange to have you left as guard for Campo Bonito when others leave— Keep me raising chickens</p>


      <p>Oh the heat Imperial Valley is a cold storage compared to Chicago— John leaves for Kansas City to night.</p>	


      <p>May be a big change in the show when we reach Denver—</p>

      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Love to all</span>
         <br/>Governor
</span>

      <p>Too hot. </p>
