      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">Beck</span>
      </span>


      <p>I hope this may find you and yours well and happy. I am in excellent health— And business splendid. I wrote you from N. Y. So did Mr Clark<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> What the Hearst Estate<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> would do—</p>

      <p>Please let me hear from you on the subject. </p>

      <p>Hope there <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="won't​ ">wont</a>
    ​ be so much trouble about our ditch this summer.</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>

   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Oswestry, England, on May 11, 1904.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: "Mr Clark" is Edward Hardy Clark (1864-1945), the financial manager for Phoebe Hearst. </div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: The Hearst Estate provided an indemnity bond of $30,000 at 7.5% interest for the Shoshone Irrigation Company project.</div>
