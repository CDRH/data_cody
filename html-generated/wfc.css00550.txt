      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>

Liverpool—<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a>
      <br/>

May. 15<u>th</u> / 03.<a href="../doc/" class="internal_link">2</a>
      <br/>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">George</span>
      </span>


      <p>How are they coming? Hope you are well and happy. I have had a tough time with a sprained ankle and shoulder. and the worst weather England ever had— and thats saying a heep. Have been looking for your report for quite a while. Would like to know about the Shoshone Irrigation Co— business. how the New Canal is. how much land sold &amp;c. If you will have money to meet the Hearsts<a href="../doc/n03" class="internal_link">3</a> interest &amp;c—</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Kind wishes</span>


         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>

   </div>



   <div class="note">Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Liverpool, England, May 4-23, 1903.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: The date inscribed is May 15, 1903.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 3: Phoebe Apperson Hearst (1842-1919) was the wife of California senator and Comstock Lode magnate George Hearst. The Hearsts provided an indemnity bond of $30,000 at 7.5% interest for the Shoshone Irrigation Company project.</div>
