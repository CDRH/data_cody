      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>

Liverpool—<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
      <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">[1]</a>
      <br/>

May. 15<u>th</u> / 03.<a name=".ref"> </a>
      <a href="#.note" class="ref">[2]</a>
      <br/>

      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>
      </span>


      <p>How are they coming? Hope you are well and happy. I have had a tough time with a sprained ankle and shoulder. and the worst weather England ever had— and thats saying a heep. Have been looking for your report for quite a while. Would like to know about the Shoshone Irrigation Co— business. how the New Canal is. how much land sold &amp;c. If you will have money to meet the Hearsts<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a> interest &amp;c—</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Kind wishes</span>


         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>

   </div>
