   <h3>
      <a class="persNameLink" href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/whitley.j">Whitley, John Robinson, 1843-1922</a>
   </h3>
   <p>
      <div class="note">John Robinson Whitley (1843-1922) was  an English entrepreneur and philanthropist. He was Executive Commissioner of the 1887 American Exhibition in London and founder of Earls Court Exhibition Centre. On an 1886 visit to New York, Whitley met with Cody and Salsbury and invited them to partner with the American Exhibition. He would later organize similar programs for Germany, Italy, and France.</div>
