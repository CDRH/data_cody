      <h6 class="fw">EVENING MAIL.</h6>
      <h6 class="fw">MANCHESTER, WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 8.</h6>

      <p>BUFFALO BILL'S WILD WEST.—An event of an especially interesting character came off at Buffalo Bill's "Wild West" last night, when one of the Indian women gave birth to what we understand is the first Sioux infant born in this country.</p>
