      <span class="salute">My Dear Elwell<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">[2]</a>
      </span>


      <p>I thought sure you would let me know how you made out with Mr Haggard<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">[3]</a>. I seen him. and he. said he liked your work and would write you. I want you to do the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="illustrating​ &#xA;    ">illustrateing</a>. And I am here <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="writing​ &#xA;    ">writeing</a> about 9000 words a day and would like to talk over the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="illustrating​ &#xA;    ">illustrateing</a>. But as you <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ &#xA;    ">dont</a> write me. I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="don't​ &#xA;    ">dont</a> know who the illustrator will be<a name="n04.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n04.note" class="ref">[4]</a>—</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Love to all</span>
         <br/>Col
</span>


      <p>You might write a few lines to<br/>

16. Ferdinand Place
New Rochelle
N.Y.

</p>
