      <span class="dateline">Chicago Dec 20th 191_ </span>

      <span class="salute">Dear John-</span>


      <p>Col wants you to have his saddle and winchester gun packed &amp; be ready to come to Crawford Nebr on a minutes notice</p>


      <p>See Mr Tammen and have him give you permission to take 150 rounds of that blank ammunition for the Colonels winchester</p>
