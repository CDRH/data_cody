                <h3>FILM PROMOTERS SUED</h3>
                <h3>Bills (Buffalo and Pawnee) Allege Breach of Contract.</h3>
                <p>NEW YORK, April 10. — (Special.) — The suit of Colonel William F. Cody ("Buffalo Bill") and Colonel Gordon W. Lillie ("Pawnee Bill") to recover $25,000 from Patrick T. Powers and Pliny R. Craft for alleged breach of contract in a moving picture show of scenes in the Wild West is being heard before the Supreme Court Justice Bijur.</p>
                <p>The two "Bills" were to make moving pictures of Wild West scenes and pose for $25,000 and a percentage of the profits.  It is charged that they are not receiving their share of the profits.</p>
