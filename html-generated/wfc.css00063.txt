      <span class="salute">My Dear O Dwyer</span>


      <p>Have written Friend Bell— Am glad you have got there. Now you old mule skinner. if You dont prove your self a good man— You will be tied behind a wagon by the thumbs and <span class="unclear illegible">[led?]</span> for three days—</p>

         <span class="salute">Regards to family<br/>

Your friend</span>
