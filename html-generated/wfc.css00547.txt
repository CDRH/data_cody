      <div class="note">Buffalo Bill's Wild West</div>


      <span class="dateline">Fairbury Neb<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a>
         <br/>


Aug 2d 1902</span>


      <span class="salute">Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/beck.g">George</a>
         </span>—</span>


      <p>Glad to get your letter. hope you can meet me in Cheyenne<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a>. The money for the extension &amp; reconstructing our Canal is just as good as though it was in the bank. So I am informed by <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/bleistein.g">Bleistein</a>
         </span>. <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/rumsey.b">Rumsey</a>
         </span> &amp; <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/gerrans.h">Gerrans</a>
         </span>. <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://codyarchive.org/doc/salsbury.n">Salsbury</a>
         </span> as you know is <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="always​ &#xA;    ">allways</a> ready— and my money is ready— go right a head— have no fear about the money part. I sent a letter to the Cody City Council. Wish you would hurry it up— I have got to build a sewer for the hotel— and have no time to <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="lose​ &#xA;    ">loose</a>— No one more than your self knows the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="benefit​ &#xA;    ">benifit</a> that hotel will be to the town. And I want to get it opened just as soon as possible—</p>


      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">Yours truly</span>


         <br/>W. F. Cody</span>

   </div>
