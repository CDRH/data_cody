      <span class="dateline">

North Platte, Neb,

Apr. 26th 1886
</span>
      <span class="salute">My Dear <span class="persName">
            <a href="http://rosie.unl.edu/cody_archive/doc/moses.m">Mollie</a>
         </span>—</span>


      <p>Enclosed find a P.O. for $25.<a name="n01.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n01.note" class="ref">1</a> I presume you are getting about ready to come to St. Louis. Wish you would start from home in time to arrive in St. Louis about the 2nd or <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="3rd​ &#xA;    ">3th</a> of May. Go to the. St. James Hotel if I <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="ain't​ &#xA;    ">aint</a> there to meet you. I will be there any how by the <a rel="tooltip" class="sic" title="3rd​ &#xA;    ">3th</a>.<a name="n02.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n02.note" class="ref">2</a> I have got you the white horse. and a fine saddle. Suppose you have your habit.<a name="n03.ref"> </a>
         <a href="#n03.note" class="ref">3</a>
      </p>

      <p>Will be glad to see you—</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <span class="salute">With Love.</span>

         <br/>W. F. C.
</span>
