      <span class="dateline">Cody. Wyo.<br/>
Feb. 12th</span>

      <span class="salute">Dear Tait.</span>


      <p>General Miles has approved the Pictures. Spoor telegraphs come at once and go to Washington.</p>

      <p>So I am starting. Will be at Hotel La Salle Chicago. Saturday—</p>

      <p>Say. Ewing writes we are now ready for the Edison Mill.</p>

      <p>You call on General ________  The Mining Editor of the Post. Any one will show you his room He can tell you what H. B. Clifford of the Edison are doing in Col-Or. May you can find out if any other process has been discovered for the treatment of our kind of concentrates.</p>

      <p>Cheer up looks like we will soon be doing business</p>
      <span class="closer">
         <br/>Gov
</span>
