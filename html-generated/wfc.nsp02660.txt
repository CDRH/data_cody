      <h3>NOTES AND OPINIONS.</h3>

      <div class="milestone line"> </div>

      <span class="gap sampling">[sampling]</span>

      <p>The latest fad in cigarette chromos<a href="../doc/n01" class="internal_link">1</a> is a picture of the Prince of Wales, John L. Sullivan<a href="../doc/n02" class="internal_link">2</a> and Buffalo Bill, with arms linked, each smoking a cigarette.</p>


      <span class="gap sampling">[sampling]</span>


   </div>




   <div class="note">Note 1: "Chromos" refers to the method known as chromolithography in which a picture is printed using the first true multi-color printing.</div>

   <div class="note">Note 2: John Lawrence Sullivan (1858-1918) is recognized as the last bare-knuckle boxing heavyweight champion, the first gloved boxing Heavyweight Champion from 1882-1892, and the first American athlete to earn over a million dollars.</div>
