Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined With Pawnee Bill's Great Far East
Carrollton Mo Sep. 251 Dear Clarence

Col Dyer was with Me when your good letter came. And he appreciated when you said we would cut out the Wyoming hunt if our mines. demanded our attention He has a monied friend in Boston he has invited to go out with us as soon as he hears from him he will get you two together

Hastly Yours Col.

We cant get Deer Bear. & quails ne at Campo Bonito2

Col Dyer-uses a No 12 Winchester Pump shot gun3
Note 1: Cody did not include the year in his dateline. Buffalo Bill's Wild West Combined With Pawnee Bill's Great Far East performed in Carrollton, Missouri, on September 25, 1911.
Note 2: Campo Bonito was a mine located about 43 miles from Tucson in Pima County, Arizona.
Note 3: This sentence is written upside down, above the letterhead imprint.
