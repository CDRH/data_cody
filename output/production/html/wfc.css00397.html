Cody. Wyo.
Feb. 12th Dear Tait.

General Miles has approved the Pictures. Spoor telegraphs come at once and go to Washington.

So I am starting. Will be at Hotel La Salle Chicago. Saturday—

Say. Ewing writes we are now ready for the Edison Mill.

You call on General ________ The Mining Editor of the Post. Any one will show you his room He can tell you what H. B. Clifford of the Edison are doing in Col-Or. May you can find out if any other process has been discovered for the treatment of our kind of concentrates.

Cheer up looks like we will soon be doing business
