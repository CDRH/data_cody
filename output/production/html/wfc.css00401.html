North Yakima Wash
June 1st 1914 [1] To Whom this May Concern

That John Tait, Has been with me for years and has held many trust worthy positions. And filled them honorably And ably— He is leaving me of his own accord— And I wish him every success. And happiness [2]

W. F. Cody
"Buffalo Bill"

Note 1: Cody performed with the Sells-Floto Circus in North Yakima, Washington, on June 1, 1914. [back]

Note 2: This recommendation is significantly more personal and complimentary to Tait than an earlier document signed by Cody and dated October 14, 1899. [back]
