Apr. 22 1886 My. Dear. Mollie

I will arrive in St Louis May 3th. Now I think it will be just as well for you to arrive there about the same time go to the St James Hotel if I dont Meet you at the Depot. and let me know you are there I hope you are well and happy. I have the white horse for you—

With Love W. F. C.
 

Scout's Rest Stock Ranch,

Hon. W. F. Cody, Proprietor. C. A. Dillon , Foreman.

North Platte, Neb.

North Platte 26 Apr 1886

Miss. Mollie Moses

Morganfield

K. Y.
