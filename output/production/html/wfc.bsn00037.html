279 Liberty Street Long Branch, NJ June 18 th /06 Mr Bronson Rumsey My dear Sir,

I herewith acknowledge receipt of check for $1000—. which I send in Mr Bleistein's care not having your present address. Thanking you for same I am very truly yours

Mrs Nate Salsbury

30 W. 96 th St. New York E. B. Allan Sheridan Wyo.

 

Mrs. Salsbury
