Feb. 28, 1917. Mr. H. M. Gerrans, Hotel Iroquois, Buffalo, N.Y. Dear Mr. Gerrans:

We enclose Mr. Bleistein's statement, $4077.43, which included interest to February 27, 1917. Mr. Bleistein will find this statement correct.

We have had a very cold bad winter, However, it looks now as though the weather conditions will permit business to open up. It has been very quiet during the extremely cold weather.

With kind regards I remain,
