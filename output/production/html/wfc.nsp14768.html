HARD LUCK FOR THE WILD WEST.

It Didn't Make a Go in Spain—Orator Frank Richmond's Death.

THE SUN received this letter yesterday:

TO THE EDITOR OF THE SUN—Sir: Buffalo Bill's show has closed here. No business. Three of the Indians died, and one has small-pox. The company is demoralized. Buffalo Bill has gone to England; don't know what the rest will do yet. Frank Richmond, the orator, died on Jan. 3 of typhoid fever. Many of the company will return to America. YOURS,

JAS. S. PARKS, Wild West. BARCELONA, SPAIN, JAN. 7.
