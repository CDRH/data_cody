July 23, 1907. Bronson Rumsey, Trustee, 78 Exchange St., Buffalo, New York. Dear Sir:

We hand you herewith the usual semi-annual statement of business transacted with respect to the Cody, Wyoming, property held jointly by this Company, and a number of persons for whom you act as trustee, for the six months ending June 30, 1907.

We also enclose draft, your order, for $718.57, for one-half of the net proceeds due you.

Please receipt and return the accompanying voucher.

I trust you will find the statement satisfactory.

Yours truly, ABMinor
Secy.
