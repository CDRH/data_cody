The "Wild West" End.

YOU bet we reckon Colonel Cody By this time quite a Glasgow body, His term of fourteen weeks, or so, In Duke Street he with plucky "go" Has undergone, and so is free Straight westward to go to tea. To keen-eyed shots—no joke if foes!— Buck-jumpers working hard for "throws," Swart Indians, Cowboys, buffaloes, And "Bill" brave captain of the crew We raise our hat, and wave "Adieu."
