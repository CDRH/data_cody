William F. Cody, mounted on gray horse in arena center and holding loving cup in right hand, addresses citizens of Clinton, Iowa, seated in grandstand; gentleman and children stand by flag-draped table in front of Cody. Hand printed on mat below photograph "Col. W. F. Cody thanking the citizens of Clinton, Iowa, after the presentation of the loving cup - Aug 4 - 1911." On verso "Irving R. Bacon, Redford, Mich."
