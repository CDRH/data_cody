Apl 6, 1929 Mr. Bronson Rumsey Buffalo, N. Y. Dear Sir:

On behalf of Mrs. Rachel Salsbury I acknowledge as per your request of April 2 receipt thereof.

Mrs. Salsbury, whose address is 1185 East 21st St, Brooklyn N.Y. is recovering from a severe attack of illness and at present I am taking care of her business   matters. Will you be so kind as to send me copies of communications to her or else write me direct, as you prefer.

Very truly yours Nate Salsbury
