The Indians belonging to the Wild West show at Earl's-court were entertained on Tuesday by Mr. Harry Atkinson, who caused considerable wonderment amongst the redskins by his vocal imitations of musical instruments, birds, and animals. On Friday Mrs. Ricks, better known as "Aunt Martha," accompanied by the Liberian Minister and others, lunched at the International Horticultural exhibition, Major Burke doing the honours on behalf of the "Wild West," which the party afterwards visited.
