Buffalo Bill's Wild West

Lynchburg. Va [1]
Apr. 24 th 99 My Dear Beck

Ex Gov. Richards [2] says we ought to fix up our affairs so that our Settlers and our selves can proove up. by doing this our company can get in some ready cash. let me hear from you, and when you expect have water runing into Cody. for goodness sakes George rush it a long—

Yours Cody

Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Lynchburg, Virginia, on April 24, 1899. [back]

Note 2: William Alford Richards (1849-1912), 4th governor of Wyoming (1895-1899) and Assistant Commissioner of the General Land Office (U.S.) during 1901. [back]
