THE EAST-END EXHIBITION BUILDING.

SIR,—Being a resident in Dennistoun, and having occasion to pass the old East-End Exhibition gates very often, the thought occurred to that it would be a good idea if the arena is which " Buffalo Bill" showed us the mysteries of the Far West was converted into a roller skating rink. It could be conducted on the same lines as the Olympia Roller Skating Rink in Great Western Road. I am sure it would be a success.— I am, &c.,

ROLLER SKATES. Glasgow, 16th September, 1892.
