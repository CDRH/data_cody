Can you use this picture in your Paper when the Poney express comes there

Ajo. 8/31 1923 Mr. H. H. Tammen My Dear Mr. Tammen

Inclose a picture of three of the orignal Ponney Express riders Col Cody and two moor allso the spot in St. Joe Mo. whare the first start was made the grave you see is whare the first man to carrey the mail is buried he started from the spot the monument is on the one on the right of the Col. took the 2 relay & the other on the left allso was a carrier this was taken in 1915 it is the only one I have Please sent it back

Best regards to you and yours J. H. Tait Ajo Arz
