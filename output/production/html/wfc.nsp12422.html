Among Her Majesty's visitors have been Prince Ferdinand of Roumania and the Duke of Aosta, with both of whom our Sovereign was greatly pleased; so perhaps that is why she treated them to selected Cossacks, Indians, Cowboys, and Gauchos from the "Wild West" on Saturday afternoon.

Note: Prince Emanuele Filiberto (1869-1931), 2nd Duke of Aosta, was a general in the Italian Army and a member of the House of Savoy.
