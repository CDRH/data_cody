Portrait of Johnny Baker holding a rifle and John Boyer reclining in front of a row of seven standing cowboys. James (Jim Kidd) Willoughby stands second on the left. The men hold lariats and wear hats, kerchiefs, pistols in belts and chaps. Handwritten caption on top of mat, "3 4 Jim Kid Willoughby 5 Billy Hanson 6 Dick Johnson 7 Long Haired Hank 8 9" Handwritten caption on bottom of mat, "1 Baker 2 John Boyer 1886-87"
