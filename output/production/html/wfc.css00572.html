Note 1: Telegram is handwritten by clerk or William F. Cody assignee.
Note 2: "Perkins" is Charles E. Perkins (1840-1907) who was a businessman and president of the Chicago, Burlington and Quincy Railroad.
Note 3: "Stokes" is likely Edward S. Stokes (1841-1901) who was at one time a railroad and oil magnate and businessman; Stokes was an owner of the Hoffman House, an elegant hotel in Manhattan.
