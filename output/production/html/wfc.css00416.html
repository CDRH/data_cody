Chicago Dec 20th 191_ Dear John-

Col wants you to have his saddle and winchester gun packed & be ready to come to Crawford Nebr on a minutes notice

See Mr Tammen and have him give you permission to take 150 rounds of that blank ammunition for the Colonels winchester
