May 22, 1913. Mr. Geo. Bleistein, Buffalo, N. Y. Dear Mr. Bleistein:-

I have your telegram and enclose a bill for the purchase price of the dog, $10.00 and express charges. I thought I would prepay wxpress charges so that you would not be bothered at that end. I hope he arrives there in good condition. The man I bought the dog from happens to be out in the hills and I will be unable to get the pedigree for possibly a week or 10 days. As soon as I get it will send it along.

Yours truly, CODY TRADING COMPANY Manager.
JMS-HT
