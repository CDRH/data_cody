New York,

April 2, 1902 [1] H. C. Alger Esq [2] Sheridan Wyo. Dear Sir

At a meeting of the Board of Directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Co held April 1st 1902 at the Hoffman House, City of New York, Mr. George T. Beck was elected Treasurer of the Company for the next ensuing year. We therefore request you as former Treasurer to deliver to Mr. Beck, all books records papers, contracts monies and other property of the company in your possession.

Yours truly W. F. Cody
President

Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West is at winter quarters in Bridgeport, Connecticut, from October 30, 1901 through April 20, 1902. [back]

Note 2: Letter is handwritten by clerk or William F. Cody assignee; "W. F. Cody" signature is Cody's. [back]
