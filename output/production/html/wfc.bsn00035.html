December 14, 1905. Mr. Bronson Rumsey, Trustee, 78 Exchange Street, Buffalo, N. Y. Dear Sir:

By request of Col. Cody, we send you herewith draft on the Chemical National Bank of New York No. 391544 of the First National Bank of this city for $11,000 as advance payment on proceeds of the one-half interest in the Cody property held by you as Trustee. A final accounting with statement andremittance will be made at the usual time at the close of the year. Be kind enough to receipt and return the accompanying voucher.

Yours truly, ABMinor
Sec'y

Jan. 18, 1906. Mr. Bronson Rumsey, 78 Exchange Street, Buffalo, New York. Dear Sir:

I hand you herewith statement of account of the one-half interest in the Cody, Wyoming property represented by you, showing all transactions for the six months ending Dec. 30, 1905.

The net balance for your half interest for that time is $12679.05, of which we remitted you $11,000.00, on the 14th ult. We now hand you herewith draft to your order for $1679.05, the balance. Please return voucher.

Very truly yours, ABMinor
Sec'y
