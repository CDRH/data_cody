Buffalo Bill's Wild West
St Paul1
July 252 Dear George

Your tel3 just recd You know the necessity of getting that Canal extended You know the Co have told you to go ahead— Money ready— if you dont get it done— And by contract— it will be a great hurt to our co— And a draw back to country & town

Its now up to you—

Kind regards W. F. Cody
Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in St. Paul, Minnesota, on July 25, 1902.
Note 2: The year is not inscribed by Cody but is 1902.
Note 3: Telegram.
