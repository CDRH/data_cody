St Louis Mo Cody, Wyoming, Apr 30 191__ Dear Tait—

Fine weather. A dandy. Show— on Preparedness which catches the people— We are on a fine Base Ball Park— Use their grand stand which seats 15000 people we have had her nearly full to d twice to day sunday will fill it—

Ringlings hammering us. But this word Preparedness draws like a plaster—

You let me hear from you savey?

I am feeling splendid

Governor
