His Highness the Maharajah Gaekwar of Baroda, G.C.S.I., accompanied by Rajashri V. M. Sanarth and Dr. Nevins visited on Saturday afternoon the Horticultural Exhibition, and were present at the performance of Buffalo Bill' "Wild West," where they were received by Major Burke.His Highness was greatly pleased with the show, and was afterwards conducted to Colonel Cody's tent, where he and his party remained for some time. On leaving the gardens the party went to the Kildare amateur athletic association.
