Otakte, or "Kill Many," one of Buffalo Bill's Sioux, who returned from Europe on Saturday seriously injured by a fall from his horse while with the show in Berlin, died in Bellevue Hospital on Tuesday. His body will be taken to the Sioux reservation by "Hank" Clifford [1] and George C. Crager, who were with Buffalo Bill as interpreters.

Note 1. "Hank" Clifford is not further identified. [back]
