Washington, D.C., Feb 23, 19__ Dear Tait.

Only a line. Thursday evening we show the pictures to the President. Secretaries &c—

Friday Matinee to Press, Army and others. Monday Mar 2d open at Lyceum theatre New York for real Money. I am to lecture on the pictures first week in New York.

If you are broke ask Mr Tammen for a loan.

In haste Governor
The New Willard
Pennsylvania Avenue, 14th and F Streets
Washington, D. C.
F. S. Hight, Manager.
