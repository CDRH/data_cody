Grand Hotel Alfred Hauser D. Coen & CI. — Milano

Naples le Feb. 15th 1890 My. Dear. Doctor

Your welcome favor found me all O.K. glad to hear from you We leve for Rome on Monday. have had a nice time here We have not lost a day this winter only when moooving This has been the trip of my life. & I tell you a big undertakeing to take such a big outfit into   strange countries— I know of no other managers who dare risk so much. I guess Barnum is sorry he followed the Wild West accross the Atlantic I see he closes in London to day. Well three months was a long stay for a Circus. I guess he wishes he had closed two months ago— I open in London in May—

I see the New York Sun is giveing me thunder every once in a while. what for I dont know. I only wish they would stick to the truth-

Remember me to all friends
With my best wishes from
Your friend Cody

Note: 'Doctor' is unidentified.
