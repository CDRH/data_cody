LS Chicago Ills Feb 15th 1914 John H Tait Albany Hotel Denver Colo

Leaving here tomorrow for New Willard Hotel Washington with pictures [1] did you get letter from Billings am feeling pretty well I want you with me personally this summer. [2] And dont you forget it. Ewing made another strike [3]

Governor 1140 PM

Note 1: Cody refers to his motion picture The Indian Wars. [back]

Note 2: Cody spent the summer of 1914 performing with the Sells-Floto Circus. [back]

Note 3: Cody refers to E. J. Ewing, a mining engineer engaged by Cody's partner Daniel Burns Dyer to examine Cody and Dyer's Arizona mining operations in 1912. Ewing later leased and operated some of the Cody-Dyer mine properties. [back]
