Buffalo Bill has returned to the arena of his former triumphs, his upright and handsome figure as picturesque as of yore, though his erstwhile dark locks have silvered somewhat since his first visit. The show is pretty much the same as usual. Age

BUFFALO BILL.

 has not withered his cowboys and Indians, nor has time added any variety to the entertainment, but Londoners and their country cousins will flock to Earls Court as of old, while, if we may judge from the distinguished company who welcomed him last Saturday, Colonel Cody will again be the idol of society.
