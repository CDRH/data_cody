June 26.

Her Majesty, accompanied by their Royal Highnesses Prince and Princess Christian, with her Highness Princess Victoria of Schleswig-Holstein, their Royal Highnesses Prince and Princess Henry of Battenberg, with their children, and her Grand Ducal Highness the Princess of Leiningen, witnessed yesterday afternoon, from the East Terrace of the Castle, a performance of a party of the "Wild West" Company, consisting of American Cowboys, Mexicans, Cossacks of the Caucasus, Gauchos, and Sioux Indians, under the direction of Colonel Cody.
