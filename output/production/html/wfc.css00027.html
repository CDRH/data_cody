Buck

My Children at Cody are getting up an illustrated folder of my dear old Home Ranch— No man ever looked on a prettier place. Its the greatest hunting and fishing. place on earth— I want you to write. Fred H. Garlow. Cody Wyo. To send you some folders—

And that you can and will send them some Summer guests and will be there your self with a party this fall—

Now Buck you can do this. And you do it. See! And do it while the trail is hot. Do you get me—

Ill sure show Greensburg with my own[?] Show this Summer. And Ill boom your game—You get busy & do as much for me—

Bill
