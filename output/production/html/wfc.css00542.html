Buffalo Bill's Wild West
Newton Ia1
Aug. 11th 1901 My Dear Beck

The Directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Co— wish me to call a meeting to be held in Buffalo the last week in August— And want both your self and Alger present— with all Books. So please send out a call for any day of that week after Monday the 26 th let me know by return mail what date you make for meeting.

Yours truly W. F. Cody President.
Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Newton, Iowa, on August 10, 1901. No performance was given on Sunday, August 11, 1901.
