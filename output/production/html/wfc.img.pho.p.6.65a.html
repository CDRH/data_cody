Embossed caption on mat, "W. & D. Downey 57 & 61 Ebury Street, London, S. W. and Newcastle-Upon-Tyne. Photographers to the Queen." Handwritten on verso, "To My Pa and Ma from their Sons Andrew and John taken in the American Exhibition London April 28th 1887. Providence R. I. July 26th 1944 There-by give this photo, of Buffalo Bills Wild West Cow Boy Band to my nephew, John M. Link, take good care of this photo, this is the only one in existence Your Uncle Andrew Link in his 81st year."
