La Salle at Madison St.

Chicago July. 6 191_
Sunday Dear, Tait.

I arrange to have you left as guard for Campo Bonito when others leave— Keep me raising chickens

Oh the heat Imperial Valley is a cold storage compared to Chicago— John leaves for Kansas City to night.

May be a big change in the show when we reach Denver—

Love to all Governor

Too hot.
