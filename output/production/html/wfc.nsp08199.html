Gift from the King.

London, March 18.—King Edward has sent Col. Cody (Buffalo Bill) a handsome scarfpin with the royal cipher in diamonds, surmounted by the crown, as a souvenir of his visit last Saturday to the Wild West show. [1]

Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in London, England, from December 6, 1902, through April 4, 1903. [back]
