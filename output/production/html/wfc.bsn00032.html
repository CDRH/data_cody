August 16, 1904. Bronson Rumsey, Esq., 78 Exchange St., Buffalo, N.Y. Dear Sir:

I beg to acknowledge receipt of your letter of August 15. I am also in receipt of draft from Commonwealth Trust Co. for $30,283.88 covering amount due me account Shoshone Irrigation Co's bonds. I thank you very much for your attention to this matter, and remain,

Yours very truly, PHOEBE A. HEARST Wm B. Cravath [1]

Note 1: William B. Cravath (1868-1951) was business secretary for the Hearst Estate. [back]
