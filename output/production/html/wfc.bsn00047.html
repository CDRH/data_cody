July 17, 1908. Mr. Bronson Rumsey, 78 Exchange St., Buffalo, N. Y. Dear Sir:

We enclose herewith statement of business transacted by this Company with respect to the half interest held by you as Trustee in the town of Cody. Wyoming, during the six months ending June 30, 1908, the same showing a balance of $24.90, which stands to the credit of your account. Same will be included in the next semi-annual statement.

Yours truly, A. B. Minor
