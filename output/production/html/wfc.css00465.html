Ralph Kitchen, Manager.
The Paxton, Omaha, Neb.
Mar. 17th 1896 My Dear Alger

Please send me a copy of W. E. Hymers expense account and money he received. He gave it to Beck last fall. Send it to me to North Platte1—

Very truly yours W. F. Cody
Note 1: Scout's Rest Ranch was William F. Cody's ranch in North Platte, Nebraska.
