BUFFALO BILL and the Wild West show take their departure in a fortnight's time. Those who have not seen the novelties which have been introduced into this highly interesting picture of life in the far west will be wise in time. The Wild West show will be one of the many attractions of the great Chicago Exhibition.

BUFFALO BILL

Seems really to mean going. Messrs. Foster and Cranfield have issued a preliminary notice of the sale of 155 horses and various impedimenta. The dates are Thursday and Friday, October 13, and 14, in the Wild West arena.
