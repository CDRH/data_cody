Buffalo Bill's Wild West

New York
Mar. 7th 1901— My. Dear. Hinkle.

Your telegram came and I now I am looking for annother one saying everything is allright— W. A. Richards promised to rush papers through as soon as they got there from Lander— [1]

Hope you are well and happy— With Kind regards W. F. Cody Hoffman House— [2]
 

Buffalo Bill's Wild West

C. L. Hinkle State Land Board Cheyene Wyo.

[stamp]
Postage stamp, red, inverted

United States Postage 2 Two Cents 2

Note 1: Cody refers to the U.S. General Land Office branch located at Lander, Wyoming, which had initial jurisdiction over the various federal land claims William F. Cody filed in the Big Horn Basin. [back]

Note 2: The Hoffman House was a leading New York hotel at the time. [back]
