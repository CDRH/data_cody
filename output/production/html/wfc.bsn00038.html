July 16th, 1906. Mr. Bronson Rumsey, Trustee, 78 Exchange Street, Buffalo, New York. Dear Sir:-

Herewith statement of account of the Cody property held jointly by this Company and yourself as Trustee representing certain other interests, showing net balance for the one-half represented by you $5424.79, for which amount you will find herewith New York draft payable to your order.

Please receipt and return the accompanying voucher, and oblige,

Yours truly, ABMinor
Sec'y.
