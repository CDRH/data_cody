Wild West Camp, Earl's Court, S. W.

May. 23th 92 My. Dear Cousins

This will introduce. Mrs. General Crook— A Lovely Lady. Whoom I wish you to know.

She will tell you all about your tough cousin.

Love to you both— Cousin. Will. Cody

Note: Likely J. Frank and Nellie Cody. 'Mrs. General Crook' refers to the wife of General George R. Crook (1828-1890), maiden name Mary Tapscott Dailey (1842-1895).
