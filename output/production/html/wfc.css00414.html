Department of the Interior
United States Indian Service

Pine Ridge S. D.
Feby 12 —1914 Mr Tate:

I received the copies of the Post you kindly sent me for which I thank you very much. I find there will not be enough to go around and will ask you to send me about six or eight more copies if the issue is not exausted.

There is enclosed $1.00. pay for the papers and then you and Hank go out and take something for the change.

Very Truly Yours John R. Brennan
 

Department of the Interior,
United States Indian Service,
John R. Brennan Pine Ridge Agency, S. D.
