Buffalo Bill's Wild West
Sandusky— O.1
July- 8 th 1902 My Dear Beck .

I am going to again. As I am pushed by Salsbury & Bleistein to hurry things— To see if I can hear from you— here is a telegrame that explains what the men want done who puts up the money— May I hear what you are doing— toward getting an Estimate. So we can get bids for the work. the Summer is slipping away wire me at my expense so I can reply to Salsbury & Buffalo parties2— I can see no reason why something cant be done & soon.

Yours truly W. F. Cody
Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Sandusky, Ohio, on July 8, 1902.
Note 2: "Buffalo parties" are the directors of the Shoshone Irrigation Company, including Rumsey , Bleistein, and Gerrans , all from Buffalo, N.Y.
