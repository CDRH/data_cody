Pine Ridge Agency, April [3, 1897] The Honorable Commissioner of Indian Affairs,1
Washington, D. C.

Sir:

I have the honor to enclose herewith original and duplicate copies of contracts entered into this day between Messrs. Cody & Salsbury , and 56 Indians of this reservation; each contract having been approved by me.

Very respectfully, W. H. Clapp
Captain 16th Infantry,
Acting Indian Agent.
Note 1: Daniel M. Browning (1846-1903) was Commissioner of Indian Affairs from April 18, 1893, to May 3, 1897.
