Altoona Sep 7th 1 Dear George —
Yours 5th to [simple drawing of hand]2 allright. No I dont see how Cody City can get a long without a gin mill & Bob3 would be a Dandy. only he would keep me full all the time. But as you dont drink it would make no difference to you— Now George— Dont over look a thing wire me from Sheridan about joint stock Co— Let me know about contract for our land from State Land Board
