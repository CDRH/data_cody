Buffalo, N. Y., August 12, 1913 191__ Please take Notice:

That a draft drawn by Cody Trading Co. on George Bleistein payable at . . . . . . . . . . . . . for Fifteen hundred and 00/100 DOLLARS, ($, 1,500.00 ) . . . . . . . . . . . dated August 5th 1913 and endorsed by you, is PROTESTED for non-payment and that the holders look to you for payment thereof. Payment having been demanded and refused.

Respectfully yours, HENRY J. AUER,
Notary Public,
At MARINE NATIONAL BANK OF BUFFALO. To Cody Trading Co.
All enclosures herewith should be sent by mail at once to the parties to whom they are directed.
