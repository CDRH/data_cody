June. 2 d 99 My Dear Beck

Every day now every mail every telegrame Boy I see- I say there is water running in the streets of Cody.

Say. I think we should fix the road through to Frosts2 I understand it wont cost over $25—

Yours in haste Cody
Note 1: Buffalo Bill's Wild West performed in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, from May 29 to June 3, 1899.
Note 2: "Frosts" may refer to property owned by Mahlon Frost (1839-1929), located on Sage Creek east of Cody, Wyoming. Cody is suggesting that road grading be completed to the edge of that property.
