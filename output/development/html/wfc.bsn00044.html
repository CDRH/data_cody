January 18, 1908. Mr. Bronson Rumsey , Trustee, Onota Farm, Pittsfield, Mass. Dear Sir:-

We hand you herewith statement of business transacted by the Lincoln Land Company at Cody for the six months ending December 31, 1907 in which you hold a one-half interest as Trustee. You will see form the statement that there is a balance of $66.49 due this Company from you as trustee.

Yours very truly, ABMinor
