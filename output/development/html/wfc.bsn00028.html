July 9, 1903. Mr. Bronson Rumsey , Trustee 78 Exchange St., Buffalo, N. Y.

Dear Sir:- We hand you herewith the semi-annual statement of business transacted at Cody with respect to the half interest represented by yourself, as Trustee, which shows a net balance due your half interest of $1748.13, voucher and draft for which amount is also enclosed. Please sign and return the voucher, and oblige.

Yours truly, ABMinor
Sec'y.
