Feb 19th 188 My Dear. Old Pard. Jim

Thanks for your letter. I have only time to say that I close here the 22.nd My winter success has been my greatest victory yet I could stay here in New York another Year. if I wished every seat in this immense building is now sold for the rest of the time I am here. & I have got the money in a Bank— Jim old Boy if my health holds out three years   I will down every showman in the world. have got them downed and I will have money to throw at the birds— then I am comeing to see you—

Regards to all— Bill. Cody

I go out to my Ranche then sail for Europe

Bill

Come over to England & see me

Buffalo Bill's Wild West New York Address: Madison Square Garden Wm. F. Cody, Nate Salsbury, Proprietors John M. Burke, General Manager
James. Geary Newton Kansas
New York, Feb 19, 6 PM F F
